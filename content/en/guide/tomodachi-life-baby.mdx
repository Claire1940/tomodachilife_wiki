<Section icon={Shield} title="The Final Decision: Islander or Traveler?">

When the **tomodachi life baby** reaches full growth, the parents will sit you down for a serious discussion. You must decide the child's future. This is a permanent decision that affects your island's population and your connectivity with other players.

### Option 1: Move into Apartments
If you choose this, the child becomes a permanent resident of your island. They will get their own room in the Mii Apartments and function like any other Mii. They can get jobs, make friends, and eventually get married themselves.

### Option 2: Send as a Traveler
If you choose this, the Mii will leave the island to travel to other players' islands via StreetPass. They will send you letters and photos from their journey, and occasionally return for visits. This is the only way to interact with the "Traveler" mechanic in Tomodachi Life.

| Choice | Pros | Cons |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Islander** | Increases island population; keeps the family together. | Takes up a valuable apartment slot. |
| **Traveler** | Allows for StreetPass interaction; unique rewards. | The Mii is no longer a permanent resident. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Strategy">
If your apartments are nearly full, sending the child as a traveler is a great way to keep the game active without hitting the population cap. However, if you have a favorite Mii combination, you'll likely want to keep them as an islander!
</Callout>
