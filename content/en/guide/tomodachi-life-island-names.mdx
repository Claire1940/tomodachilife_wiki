## FAQ

**Q: Can I change my Tomodachi Life island names after starting the game?**

A: In the original 3DS version, the island name is permanent once set. However, in the 2026 *Living the Dream* edition, you can change the name once per month by visiting the Town Hall, though it costs a significant amount of in-game currency.

**Q: Does the island name affect gameplay or Mii behavior?**

A: No, the name is purely cosmetic. It does not change the items available in the "Rite Price" store or the frequency of Mii News updates. It only affects the text displayed in menus and dialogue.

**Q: Are there any forbidden words for island names?**

A: Yes, Nintendo employs a profanity filter for all **tomodachi life island names**. If you attempt to use a name that contains blacklisted words, the game will prevent you from proceeding. This is especially strict in the 2026 version due to the enhanced online sharing features.

**Q: How do I get my island name to show up on the Mii News Tower?**

A: The Mii News Tower automatically pulls your island name for various headlines. To see it more often, ensure you are checking the news twice a day (morning and evening) for the latest updates on your residents.
