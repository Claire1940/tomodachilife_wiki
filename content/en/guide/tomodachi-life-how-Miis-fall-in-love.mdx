## The Foundation: From Strangers to Best Friends

Before two Miis can even consider a romantic relationship, they must first establish a friendship. In the world of Tomodachi Life, romance is almost always a "slow burn" that builds upon a solid social foundation. You cannot force two Miis who have never met to suddenly fall in love; they must be introduced and spend time together first.

The easiest way to start this process is to wait for a Mii to have a "thought bubble" (represented by a gray icon) asking to meet someone new. However, you can also take a more proactive approach. If you have two Miis you want to pair up, try to keep them in close proximity or interact with them frequently.

### Friendship Progression Tiers

The relationship status of a Mii can be checked in their profile. Understanding these tiers helps you gauge how close they are to a potential romance.

## The Love Bubble: Identifying a Crush

The most exciting moment in the game is when a pink heart icon appears in a Mii's apartment window. This indicates that the Mii has "something on their mind" regarding their feelings for another resident. When you click this bubble, the Mii will confess they have feelings for someone and ask for your advice.

When the Mii asks what you think, you should generally encourage them by saying "Sounds like love!" or "Go for it!" This sets the confession event in motion. At this stage, the Mii is officially "Crushing" or, if the friendship was already very high, they may be "Head over Heels."

### Moving In Together
In some versions of the game or specific scenarios, Miis may express a desire to move in together before a formal marriage. This usually happens when they are "Best Friends" and one is crushing. They will ask to move into the other's apartment or suggest a shared activity, like watching TV.

> **Warning:** If you discourage a Mii from pursuing their crush, they will enter a state of sadness. You will need to cheer them up with food, music, or new items to fill their happiness bar back up.

## The Confession: Making it Official

Once a Mii decides to confess, you get to play matchmaker by choosing the location and the style of the confession. While the location (the beach, the park, the roof) often feels like it should matter, the most important factor is the existing relationship level between the two Miis.

### Confession Styles and Outcomes

| Style | Tone | Best Used For... |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Romantic** | Soft and sweet. | Miis with "Kind" personalities. |
| **Serious** | Direct and honest. | Miis with "Reserved" or "Confident" personalities. |
| **Cute** | Playful and high-energy. | "Easygoing" or "Outgoing" Miis. |
| **Action** | Dramatic and intense. | Miis with high energy levels. |

During the confession, a third Mii might sometimes interrupt, creating a "love triangle" scenario. You will have to decide which Mii the "crushing" Mii should choose, or let them decide for themselves. If the confession is successful, the two Miis will officially start dating, and their relationship status will change to "Sweetheart."

## FAQ

**Q: Can I force two Miis to fall in love if they aren't friends yet?**

A: Not directly. They must at least be acquaintances. You can try to trigger an introduction by giving one Mii a "Rent-a-Chef" or other interactive item, but the game's AI ultimately decides who wants to meet whom.

**Q: Why did my Mii get rejected during the confession?**

A: Rejections happen if the friendship level wasn't high enough or if the other Mii is already in love with someone else. To avoid this, ensure the Miis are at least "Great Friends" before encouraging a confession.

**Q: How does personality affect tomodachi life how Miis fall in love?**

A: Personalities determine compatibility scores. For example, an "Outgoing" Mii might pair better with another "Outgoing" Mii, though "opposites attract" scenarios are also programmed into the game's logic.

**Q: Can Miis get divorced?**

A: Yes. If a married couple's relationship drops to "Unhappy" and isn't repaired through travel tickets or gifts, they may ask to get a divorce. This will result in them moving back into separate apartments and their house being removed.
