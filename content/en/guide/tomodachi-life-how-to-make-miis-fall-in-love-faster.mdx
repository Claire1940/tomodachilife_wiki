## Rerolling for Hearts: The Soft Reset Method

If you are tired of waiting for the pink heart icon to appear, you can use the "rerolling" technique. This method exploits the way the game generates "problems" or social events when you first boot it up.

### How to Reroll Romantic Events
*   **Save your game** before you plan to stop playing for a few hours.
*   When you return, **check the apartments** immediately.
*   If you don't see any pink hearts or orange "friendship" icons, **exit to the 3DS Home Menu without saving**.
*   **Restart the game.** The internal clock will often trigger new events upon the fresh load.
*   Repeat this process until the desired Mii has a heart icon.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not change the 3DS system clock to speed this up. Changing the system time will "freeze" all shop updates and events for 24-48 hours as a penalty.

## The Confession: Ensuring Success

Once you finally see that pink heart, do not click it immediately! Save your game first. This allows you to retry the confession if it fails.

When choosing a confession style, **"Be Romantic"** is statistically the most successful across various personality types, particularly for those in the "Easygoing" or "Softie" categories. If the Mii is rejected, the game sometimes offers a "Try Again" bubble. Keep tapping the Mii's thought bubble; occasionally, they will attempt a second confession immediately, which has a higher success rate.
