<Section icon={Settings} title="Core Voice Mechanics and Regional Accents">

One of the most common questions players ask is how to change a Mii's accent. Unlike physical attributes, the accent is not a setting you can toggle for individual Miis. Instead, it is tied directly to your hardware's regional configuration. This means your **tomodachi life voice guide** starts with your system settings.

| Region Setting | Accent Result | Vocabulary Style |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **United States** | American Accent | Uses "Soda," "Apartment," "Chips" |
| **United Kingdom** | British Accent | Uses "Fizzy Drink," "Flat," "Crisps" |
| **Europe (Other)** | Regional English | Varies based on specific country selection |
| **Australia** | Australian Accent | Uses localized slang and terminology |

<Callout type="warning" title="Regional Lock Warning">
Changing your system region to alter accents may affect other system-level features. In some versions, this can also trigger a 24-hour lockout on shop updates if the system clock is adjusted simultaneously.
</Callout>

If you find the synthesized voices grating or simply want a different atmosphere, the game allows for full volume control.
