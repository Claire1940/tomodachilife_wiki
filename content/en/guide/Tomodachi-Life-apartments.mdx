<Section icon={Home} title="Understanding Tomodachi Life Apartments">
Tomodachi Life apartments serve as the personal living spaces for your Mii residents. Each Mii gets their own apartment, which acts as a hub for their daily activities, interactions, and where you can observe their unique personalities and needs. These spaces are central to island life, influencing Miis' moods, relationships, and even the events that unfold.

Customizing your Miis' apartments is a key aspect of making your island truly unique. From vibrant themes to cozy room skins, the choices you make can reflect your Miis' personalities and create a more engaging environment.

### Apartment Basics
Miis begin with a basic apartment, but as you progress, more customization options become available. These options are crucial for keeping your residents happy and engaged with island life. The apartment is also where you'll respond to many of your Miis' requests, like feeding them or solving their problems.

<Section icon={Key} title="Unlocking the Interiors Shop">
The Interiors Shop is your primary source for apartment customization options. Unlocking this shop is one of the early milestones in Tomodachi Life, allowing you to start personalizing your Miis' living spaces.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Add More Miis">
    Start by adding several Miis to your island. While not directly tied to the shop unlock, a larger population accelerates overall game progression and money accumulation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Accumulate Funds">
    Ensure at least two of your Miis save a combined total of **$200 or more**. This is a critical trigger for the Interiors Shop to become available. Encourage Miis to earn money through daily activities or by solving their problems.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Solve Mii Problems">
    Actively address your Miis' various problems (indicated by thought bubbles above their apartments). Solving problems often rewards money and can help trigger shop unlocks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Check for Unlock Event">
    Once the financial condition is met, an event will trigger, announcing the grand opening of the Interiors Shop. It will appear on your island's map.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Quick Unlock Strategy">
Focus on nurturing your first few Miis by feeding them their favorite foods and resolving their issues promptly. This not only keeps them happy but also helps them save money faster, leading to an earlier Interiors Shop unlock.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Building} title="Acquiring and Applying Interiors">
Once you've unlocked the Interiors Shop, the process of acquiring and applying new apartment interiors is straightforward.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Visit the Interiors Shop">
    Head to the Interiors Shop on your island map. It typically opens after you've accumulated enough money and solved initial Mii problems.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Browse and Purchase">
    Explore the available themes and room skins. Each item will have a price. Select the one you wish to purchase for a Mii.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select a Mii">
    After purchasing an interior, you'll be prompted to choose which Mii's apartment to apply it to. Consider their personality and preferences for the best effect.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm Application">
    Confirm your choice, and the new interior will instantly be applied to the selected Mii's apartment. You can visit their apartment to see the change.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Obtain via Gifts/Events">
    Some exclusive interiors cannot be bought directly from the shop. They might be given as gifts from Miis who level up, or as rewards for participating in special island events and SpotPass distributions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Mii Preferences">
Miis have individual likes and dislikes for different interiors. Giving them an interior they dislike can negatively impact their mood, so pay attention to their reactions!
</Callout>
