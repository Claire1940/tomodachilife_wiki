### Step-by-step triage order

2. **Relaunch Tomodachi Life without multitasking**.
3. **Try to reproduce the exact bug path** (same building, same sequence).
4. **Check whether other games fail**. If yes, this may be system-level.
5. **Inspect cartridge fit and contacts** (if physical copy).
6. **Back up what you can before deeper changes**.

> **Tip:** Keep a short “glitch log” with time, location, and action sequence. This makes pattern detection much easier than memory alone.
