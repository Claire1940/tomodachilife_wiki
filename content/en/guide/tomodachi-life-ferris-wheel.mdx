<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering the 'Poke the Ferris Wheel' Mini-Game">
Once the **Tomodachi Life Ferris Wheel** is unlocked, you can participate in a fun mini-game called "Poke the Ferris Wheel." The goal is simple: poke the Ferris Wheel three times and get your Mii's gondola to stop at the very bottom. This mini-game tests your timing and power control.

### How to Play

The mini-game involves a simple yet engaging mechanic:

| Action             | Description                                                                 | Key Mechanic       |
| :----------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :----------------- |
| **Initial Poke**   | The first poke sets the wheel in motion.                                    | Timing and Power   |
| **Hold A Button**  | **Holding down the A button** increases the power of your poke.             | Power Control      |
| **Release A Button** | Releasing the A button determines the strength of the poke.                 | Precision Release  |
| **Three Pokes**    | You get three chances to adjust the wheel's rotation.                       | Strategic Poking   |
| **Goal**           | Stop your Mii's gondola at the **very bottom** of the Ferris Wheel.        | Target Accuracy    |

<Callout type="warning" title="Practice Makes Perfect">
The "Poke the Ferris Wheel" mini-game can be tricky initially. Don't get discouraged if you don't succeed on your first few tries. Pay attention to the force meter and how the wheel reacts to different levels of power.
</Callout>

### Strategy for Success

To consistently win the "Poke the Ferris Wheel" mini-game:

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Observe Momentum">
    After each poke, pay close attention to how fast the Ferris Wheel is spinning and in what direction. This will inform your next poke.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Vary Power Levels">
    Don't use maximum power every time. Experiment with short taps for small adjustments and longer holds for bigger pushes, especially on the first poke.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Anticipate Stopping Point">
    The wheel doesn't stop immediately. Try to release your poke slightly *before* the gondola reaches the bottom, allowing momentum to carry it the rest of the way.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Focus on the Gondola">
    Keep your eyes on your Mii's gondola. It's easier to judge the stopping point if you're tracking its movement rather than the entire wheel.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
