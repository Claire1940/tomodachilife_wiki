## Requirements in the Tomodachi Life Marriage Guide

Before you can even think about a wedding ceremony, certain conditions must be met. The game does not allow Miis to jump straight into marriage. The most fundamental rule is that both Miis must be in a committed relationship first. This starts when one Mii develops feelings for another and asks for your permission to confess. If the confession is successful, they will become "Sweethearts."

Age is another critical factor. In Tomodachi Life, Miis are classified as either children or adults based on the birthdate you provided. Only adult Miis can get married. If you have a pair of "Kid" Miis who are deeply in love and you want them to marry, you will need to use an **Age-O-Matic** item on both of them to turn them into adults.

| Requirement | Description | Importance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Relationship Status** | Must be "Sweethearts" and have a high relationship level. | Mandatory |
| **Age Status** | Both Miis must be adults (or aged up with an Age-O-Matic). | Mandatory |
| **Compatibility** | While not strictly mandatory, higher compatibility helps the process. | Recommended |
| **Apartment Space** | You must have room in the Mii Apartments for them to live. | Mandatory |

> **💡 Tip:** Check the Mii's relationship status screen frequently. If the status is "Let's get married!" or "Greatly in love," a proposal is likely to happen soon.

## Strengthening the Romantic Bond

Once your Miis are dating, your primary goal is to increase their relationship level. The higher the level, the more likely one Mii will bring up the topic of marriage. You can influence this by encouraging them to spend time together. If you see one Mii in another's apartment, that is a positive sign that their bond is strengthening.

Giving gifts is one of the most effective ways to boost relationship stats. Specifically, **Travel Tickets** are the gold standard for romantic progression. When you give a Travel Ticket to a Mii who is in a relationship, they will almost always take their partner with them. The resulting vacation photos signify a massive jump in their relationship bar.

| Item/Action | Effect on Relationship | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Travel Ticket** | Provides a massive boost to the bond between partners. | Use when the relationship feels "stagnant." |
| **Relationship Advice** | Choosing supportive answers when a Mii asks about their partner. | Always pick "Perfect match" or "Great." |
| **Conflict Resolution** | Resolving fights immediately before the relationship sours. | Use a "Music Box" or "Hand Mirror" to calm them. |

If a couple gets into a fight (indicated by a fire icon or a stormy cloud), you must intervene immediately. A long-lasting grudge can lead to a breakup, which will reset your progress. Talk to the Mii with the icon and help them apologize to their partner to keep the dream of marriage alive.

## The Proposal Mini-Game Explained

The most stressful part of any **tomodachi life marriage guide** is the proposal mini-game. This event is triggered when a Mii has a thought bubble with a pink heart icon and specifically says they want to propose. They will ask for your help in choosing a location (such as the Restaurant, Beach, or Fireworks display) and a romantic outfit.

Once the event begins, you enter a mini-game where you must help the Mii time their proposal perfectly. The goal is to tap the "Now!" button (or press the A button) when the partner is thinking about the Mii who is proposing. You will see thought bubbles appearing over the partner's head showing various items, other Miis, or hobbies. You must wait until a picture of the proposing Mii appears.

### Proposal Mini-Game Mechanics

| Action | Control | Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dodge Distractions** | Left Analog Stick | Moves the Mii to avoid negative thought bubbles. |
| **Clear Obstacles** | A Button / Tap Screen | Shoots or removes distracting thoughts. |
| **The Big Question** | "Now!" Button | Triggers the actual proposal attempt. |

You have three hearts (attempts) to get this right. If you miss the timing three times, the Mii will lose confidence, and the proposal will fail. If you succeed, the partner will accept, and a beautiful wedding sequence will play automatically.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not rush the "Now!" button. Wait for the partner's thought bubble to clearly show the proposing Mii for at least a full second before clicking.

## Life After the Wedding

After the successful proposal, the Miis will get married in a ceremony attended by their closest friends on the island. Following the wedding, they will go on a honeymoon to a random location (like Hawaii or Paris) and return with a souvenir for you.

The biggest change in gameplay is the introduction of **Mii Homes**. While both Miis will retain their individual apartments, they will now also share a house in the Mii Homes section of the island. They will spend most of their time there. This is also the stage where the possibility of having children arises. If you have "Babies" enabled in the Town Hall settings, a married Mii may eventually ask you if they should have a baby.

## FAQ

**Q: Can two Miis of the same gender get married in Tomodachi Life?**

A: In the original 3DS version of Tomodachi Life, same-sex marriage is not natively supported. However, players often use workarounds like creating a Mii that looks like one gender but is assigned the other in the settings. Some community mods for 2026 have also addressed this.

**Q: What happens if a Mii rejects a proposal?**

A: If the mini-game fails or the relationship isn't strong enough, the Mii will be heartbroken. You will need to comfort them with their favorite foods or items to remove the sadness bar before they can try to propose again later.

**Q: How do I get an Age-O-Matic for this tomodachi life marriage guide?**

A: The Age-O-Matic is a random reward given by Miis when they level up or when you solve their problems. It is essential if you want your younger Miis to follow the steps in this **tomodachi life marriage guide** and get married.

**Q: Can married Miis get divorced?**

A: Yes, if their relationship level drops significantly and they have a major unresolved fight, one Mii may ask to break up or divorce. This is why it is important to keep using Travel Tickets even after they are married.
