## The Rerolling Method: How to Get More Hearts

If your island has become stagnant, you can use a technique known as "rerolling." This is the most effective way to force the game to generate new social events, including those coveted pink heart icons.

1.  **Save your game** and then close it completely.
2.  Wait at least a few hours (or change the system clock, though this can temporarily disable shops).
3.  Open the game and immediately check the apartments for icons.
4.  If no hearts or friendship requests appear, **exit the game without saving**.
5.  Reload the game. The "needs" of the Miis will reset, potentially generating a new event.

This method works because the game calculates social needs upon loading the save file after a period of inactivity. By refusing to save a "boring" state, you force the AI to roll the dice again.
