## Solving Mii Problems and Requests

The most consistent way to earn happiness is by attending to the icons that appear in the apartment windows. These icons indicate that a Mii has a need or a desire that only you, the "look-alike" or island deity, can fulfill.

### Common Problem Icons
1.  **Black Scribble (Sadness):** This occurs after a breakup or a major fight. You must console the Mii by giving them items, food, or music until the sadness bar depletes.
2.  **Orange Face (General Need):** This usually involves hunger, a desire for new clothes, or a request for a specific item like a bath set or a swing.
3.  **Green Person (Friendship):** The Mii wants to become friends with someone or resolve a minor tiff.
4.  **Pink Heart (Romance):** The Mii has feelings for another and wants to confess. This provides a massive happiness boost if successful.

## Level Up Rewards and Milestones

Every time a Mii levels up, you are prompted to give them a reward. These rewards are not just cosmetic; they add new layers of gameplay and interaction to your island.

### Standard Rewards (Levels 1-20)
Between levels 1 and 20, you can choose from several categories of rewards. Each Mii can hold a limited number of these, but you can swap them out later.

*   **Gifts:** Items like skateboards, Wii Us, or sewing kits. These allow Miis to perform new activities in their rooms or at island locations.
*   **Songs:** Unlock genres like Rock, Pop, or Opera. Once learned, Miis can perform at the Concert Hall.
*   **Phrases:** Teach your Mii what to say when they are happy, angry, or worried.
*   **Interiors:** Change the wallpaper and furniture of their apartment.
*   **Pocket Money:** Give your Mii a small allowance. If they save up £10,000, they will eventually ask to go to space.

### Post-Level 20 Rewards
Once a Mii reaches Level 20, the reward system changes. Instead of the standard list, you gain the ability to give that Mii **any piece of clothing or hat** currently in your collection, regardless of whether it is sold in the shop. Additionally, the Mii may reward you with a Silver or Gold coin, which can be sold at the Pawn Shop for significant cash.

| Level Range | Available Rewards | Special Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **1 - 20** | Songs, Gifts, Phrases, Interiors | Max 8 gifts and 8 songs per Mii. |
| **21+** | All Clothing/Hats, Silver/Gold Coins | Coins are a great source of island income. |

## Advanced Happiness Strategies

To truly master **tomodachi life how to level up happiness**, you must look beyond simple feeding. Utilizing items and social dynamics can lead to faster level gains.

### Using Travel Tickets
Travel Tickets are rare items obtained through minigames or as rewards. Giving a Travel Ticket to a Mii is one of the fastest ways to boost their happiness and their relationship with another Mii. They will take a friend or partner with them to a random location (like China or Hawaii), and both Miis will return with a massive happiness increase.

### Managing Friendships
Miis who have many friends are more likely to have "Orange Face" problems, which are opportunities for you to earn XP. To increase a Mii's social circle:
1.  **Respond to friendship requests** immediately.
2.  **Use the Save Editor** (for modded 3DS users) to manually set friendships, though this carries a risk of save corruption if not done carefully.
3.  **Encourage social gatherings** by giving multiple Miis the same gift (like a Nintendo 3DS XL), which increases the chance they will play together.

> **⚠️ Warning:** While using a Save Editor can quickly maximize happiness and relationships, manually "friending" everyone at once can break the game's logic. Always back up your save file before attempting mods in 2026.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the maximum level a Mii can reach in Tomodachi Life?**

A: There is no hard cap on levels in Tomodachi Life. While the rewards change after Level 20, Miis can continue to level up into the hundreds. After Level 99, the level is displayed with a crown or special icon depending on the region.

**Q: Does the Love Compatibility Machine help level up happiness?**

A: No. The Love Compatibility Machine is purely for the player's entertainment and is based on the Miis' birthdays. It does not affect their actual relationship status or their happiness levels. To raise relationship status, use Travel Tickets or solve friendship problems.

**Q: Can I lose happiness levels if a Mii is sad?**

A: You cannot lose levels, but a Mii's "Sadness Bar" can cover their happiness bar. You must resolve the sadness (usually caused by a fight or breakup) before you can continue earning happiness XP for that Mii.

**Q: Why isn't my Mii leveling up even though I'm feeding them?**

A: If you feed a Mii the same food repeatedly, the happiness gain diminishes significantly. To effectively understand **tomodachi life how to level up happiness**, you must provide a variety of foods and items to keep the XP gains high.

**Q: What is the best gift to give a Mii for happiness?**

A: The "Travel Ticket" is objectively the best item for happiness. For standard gifts, the "Mobile Phone" or "Wii U" are excellent as they allow the Mii to call others or play games, leading to more problem icons appearing in their windows.
