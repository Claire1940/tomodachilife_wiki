<Section icon={Swords} title="Setting Up Your Multiplayer Session">

Setting up a **tomodachi life multiplayer** session is straightforward, but it requires both players to have progressed past the initial island tutorial. You must unlock the "Town Hall" and the "Port" before the multiplayer menus become accessible.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock the Port">
    Complete the first three Mii requests on your island to unlock the Port facility located on the southern coast.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enable Wireless">
    Both players must ensure their Nintendo Switch consoles have Airplane Mode turned off and Wireless communication enabled.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select Sync Mode">
    Navigate to the Town Hall and select the "Wireless Connection" option. One player chooses "Host" and the other chooses "Join."
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Exchange Data">
    Once connected, you can select which Miis or items you wish to send to your friend's island.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Fast Sync">
Using the "Quick Sync" feature at the Port allows you to trade daily items (like food or clothing) in under 30 seconds.
</Callout>

</Section>
