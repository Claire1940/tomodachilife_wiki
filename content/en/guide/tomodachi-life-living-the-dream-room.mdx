<Section icon={Map} title="Acquiring Interior Sets and Furniture">
There are two main avenues for acquiring new room interior sets in *Tomodachi Life: Living the Dream*: the T&C Reno furniture shop and the Wishing Fountain. Each offers distinct opportunities for expanding your Mii's decor options.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Shop at T&C Reno">
    Once unlocked, **T&C Reno** will feature a rotating selection of interior sets. Check back regularly for new items and weekly specials. Prices for sets like the **8-Bit Set** (188) or **Alley-Oop Set** (170) are affordable early on.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize the Wishing Fountain">
    At **Wishing Fountain Level 3**, you gain the option to wish for additional room interior sets to be added to the T&C Reno shop. This is crucial for obtaining rarer or specific themed sets, such as the **Aristocrat Set** (1,000) or **Athletic Set** (85).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Seasonal Set Acquisition">
    Certain interior sets are **seasonal** and will only appear during their corresponding time of year. Be sure to wish for these at the Wishing Fountain or purchase them from T&C Reno before the season ends, as they rotate out.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
Don't miss out on seasonal interior sets! They disappear once their season is over and may not return until the following year. Prioritize wishing for and purchasing these unique items.
</Callout>

</Section>
