<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Influencing Relationship Levels: Tips & Tricks">
Managing your Miis' relationships can be both challenging and rewarding. While some events are random, players have several ways to influence the **Tomodachi Life relationship levels**, either by strengthening existing bonds or preventing breakdowns. Understanding these mechanics is vital for a harmonious or hilariously dramatic island.

<Callout type="warning" title="Personality Matters">
Miis with similar personalities tend to get along more easily, while wildly different personalities may lead to more arguments. Consider this when creating new Miis or advising existing ones.
</Callout>

### Raising Relationship Levels

There are several reliable methods to improve the bonds between your Miis. These actions directly impact their happiness and their relationship status.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Give Positive Relationship Advice">
    When a Mii asks for your opinion on another Mii, choosing "They're great together!" will boost their bond. Selecting "They're okay" maintains the status, while "You're not compatible" will lower it. This is a simple, daily way to nudge relationships in the right direction.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Send Miis on Dates">
    For sweethearts or spouses, sending them on a date significantly improves their relationship. Dates are mini-events that, when successful, result in both Miis returning happier and with a stronger bond.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Use Travel Tickets">
    Travel tickets are powerful items that can be given to Miis. If given to a pair (sweethearts or spouses), they will go on a vacation together, automatically raising their relationship by one or two levels upon their return. This is especially useful for quickly mending strained romantic bonds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Facilitate Positive Interactions">
    Encourage Miis to spend time together. This can involve ensuring they are in the same personality group or simply responding to their requests to hang out. More positive interactions naturally lead to stronger bonds over time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Preventing Relationship Breakdowns

Relationships can also decline, leading to arguments, breakups, or even divorce. Being proactive can help you avoid these negative outcomes.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Resolve Arguments Promptly">
    If Miis have an argument, a problem bubble will appear. Intervening and helping them reconcile can prevent the relationship from worsening. Unresolved fights can cause significant drops in their **Tomodachi Life relationship levels**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Manage Confessions Carefully">
    A rejected confession can cause a Mii's relationship with the other Mii to drop by up to three levels. While you can't guarantee acceptance, being aware of the Miis' existing bond and personalities can give you a better idea of likely outcomes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Address Breakup Requests">
    If a sweetheart asks to break up, you can advise them to "work things out." If successful, their negative dating status will reset to its default level, giving them a fresh start. This recovery rule, however, does not apply to spouses seeking divorce.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Consider Player-Related Miis">
    Miis marked as being related to the real-life player will not date the player's look-alike Mii and are less likely to fight with them. This can be a useful setting for maintaining certain island dynamics.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

The Compatibility Tester is a valuable building in Tomodachi Life that provides insights into how well Miis might get along. It's not a guarantee, but it's an excellent forecast tool to help you plan social interactions and potential romantic pairings.

### How the Compatibility Tester Works

| System Feature            | Requirement                                      | Result                                                      | Why It Matters                                                |
| :------------------------ | :----------------------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Access Unlocked**       | At least 2 male Miis, 2 female Miis, 30 solved problems | The Compatibility Tester building appears on your island.   | First tool for strategic relationship planning.               |
| **Birthday Rule**         | Both Miis must have full birthdays saved.        | Without birthdays, compatibility cannot be tested.          | Mandatory input for accurate forecasts.                       |
| **Friendship Forecast**   | Same-gender, family, or different age-group opposite-gender pairs (Western releases) | Shows next week's outlook for Work/Study, Chats, Leisure.   | Helps decide who to encourage for best-friend routes.         |
| **Romance Forecast**      | Opposite-gender pairs in the same age group (Western releases) | Adds "Love" forecast alongside other weekly categories.     | Crucial for identifying potential romantic pairings.          |
| **Forecast Scale**        | Weekly categories use weather-style ratings.     | Ranges from full sun (best) to full storm (worst).          | Provides a quick visual read of relationship outlook.         |
| **Personality Influence** | Personality group matters before and during social development. | Similar personalities often mesh better than different ones. | Strong planning signal for building specific island dynamics. |

Even in the whimsical world of Tomodachi Life, relationships can face challenges. Breakups and divorces are possible, but players often have a chance to intervene or understand the mechanics behind them.

### Breakdown Mechanics

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Arguments and Fights">
    Frequent arguments without resolution can severely damage **Tomodachi Life relationship levels**. If a fight escalates and isn't resolved, the Miis may be removed from each other's relationship list, reverting to mere acquaintances.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Failed Confessions">
    A rejected confession is a significant blow. It can cause both Miis' relationship status to drop by as many as three levels, making it harder for them to recover romantically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Breakup Requests (Sweethearts)">
    If a sweetheart Mii asks to break up due to low relationship levels, you can advise them to "work things out." A successful reconciliation will reset their negative dating status to its default level, giving them another chance.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Divorce Requests (Spouses)">
    Married Miis can also request a divorce if their relationship deteriorates. Unlike sweetheart breakups, there's no automatic reset for married status, making divorce a more permanent split.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="warning" title="Parental Protection">
Married Miis will not fight or divorce while they are actively raising a child at home. This provides a temporary period of stability for the family unit.
</Callout>
