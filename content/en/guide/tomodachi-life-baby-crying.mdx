<Section icon={Baby} title="Understanding the Crying Mechanic">

When your married Mii couple decides to have a child, they enter the parenting phase. During the first three days of the child's life, the game may trigger a "crying baby" event, requiring you to intervene. This is not a random glitch but a core social interaction that deepens the bond between the parents and the child.

**Key Facts About Baby Crying:**
- The crying event is exclusive to the first three days of the baby's life.
- Parents will only request your help for crying episodes before 8:00 PM.
- Responding to the crying helps maintain the family's happiness levels.
- You must be present in the game to address the problem bubble when it appears.
