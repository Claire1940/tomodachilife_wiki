## Primary Methods for Obtaining Hair Dye

Hair dye is classified as a "Special Item" (or Treasure/Gift) in the game's internal logic. Since you cannot purchase it from the Import Store or the Clothing Shop, you must rely on the gratitude of your Miis. Most items in this category are granted as rewards for interacting with your islanders and keeping them happy.

### 1. Solving Mii Problems
The most common way to receive hair dye is by solving standard Mii problems. When a Mii has a black "thought bubble" over their head in their apartment, it indicates they have a problem or a request. This could range from being hungry to needing a new outfit or wanting to play.

| Problem Type | Icon Color | Potential Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **General Need** | Black | Hair Dye, Travel Ticket, Cold Medicine |
| **Friendship/Love** | Orange/Pink | Experience, Level Up Gifts |
| **Games/Fun** | Green | Treasures, Food, or Special Items |

### 2. Winning Minigames
When a Mii has a green thought bubble, they want to play a game. If you successfully win the minigame, you are presented with three boxes (Small, Medium, and Large). Hair dye can appear in any of these boxes, though it is a randomized drop.

*   **Match-up:** Memorize the cards to find pairs.
*   **The "Who is this?" Quiz:** Identify a Mii based on a zoomed-in photo or silhouette.
*   **Catch:** Tap the screen the moment an item is dropped.

> **💡 Tip:** Always go for the Small or Medium boxes if you are hunting for consumables like hair dye. Large boxes frequently contain expensive treasures intended for selling at the Pawn Shop, rather than functional items.

## How to Use Hair Dye on Your Miis

Once you have successfully navigated the steps of **tomodachi life how to get hair dye**, you need to know how to apply it. Unlike hats or clothing, hair dye is a one-time use consumable.

1.  Enter the **Apartment** of the Mii you wish to customize.

4.  Select the **Hair Dye** and confirm you want to give it to the Mii.

## Farming Hair Dye Effectively

If you are looking to stock up on hair dye in 2026, you need to maximize the number of interactions you have per hour. The game's RNG (Random Number Generation) can be fickle, but you can tilt the odds in your favor by following these expert strategies.

### Keep Your Island Active
The more Miis you have on your island, the more problems will generate. A full island of 100 Miis will produce significantly more "black bubble" requests than a small island of 10. Ensure you are scanning QR codes to fill your apartments quickly.

### The "Fountain Donation" Strategy
Every morning, your Miis will gather at the Fountain for donations. While this usually results in cash, it also increases the happiness of the participating Miis. Happy Miis are more likely to trigger "green bubble" minigame requests later in the day.

### Leveling Up Miis
Every time a Mii levels up, you get to give them a gift. While you can't give them hair dye as a level-up reward, the act of leveling them up often triggers a "thank you" sequence where they may hand over a special item from their inventory.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not use the 3DS system clock manipulation to speed up time. This often "freezes" shop updates and donation events for 24-48 hours, actually slowing down your progress in obtaining rare items.

## How to Remove Hair Dye

Sometimes, you might regret that neon orange mohawk. Because hair dye is a special status effect, you cannot simply go into the Mii Maker to reset the color to "Natural." You have two options for removal:

1.  **Hair Color Remover:** This is another special item obtained through the same methods as hair dye (minigames and problem-solving). Giving this to a Mii will restore their original genetic hair color.
2.  **Bathtime:** Giving a Mii a "Bath Set" sometimes has a chance to reset their appearance, though this is less reliable than the dedicated remover item.

## Advanced Customization Tips

While hair dye covers the entire head of hair, remember that it does not affect eyebrows unless you manually change them in the Town Hall's Mii editing suite. For the most cohesive look, match the eyebrow color to the new dyed shade.
