<Section icon={Target} title="The Treasure Collection Flow">

Collecting items is a structured process. By following a consistent daily route, you can significantly increase your efficiency in hunting down those elusive completionist items.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Morning Island Sweep">
    Check the Mii Apartments for black problem bubbles. These are often indicators that a resident has found an item or wants to share a discovery with you.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Resident Engagement">
    Interact with residents who have reached a new level. The quality of the gift they provide is directly proportional to their current happiness and relationship status.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Port and Campground">
    Visit the Port daily. If you have active StreetPass connections, travelers will bring unique treasures from other islands that cannot be found locally.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Catalog Tracking">
    Regularly check your progress in the Town Hall. This allows you to identify which categories of treasures are still missing from your collection.
  </Step>
</Steps>
