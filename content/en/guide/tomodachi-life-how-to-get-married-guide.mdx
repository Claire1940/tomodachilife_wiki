## Step 1: Setting the Foundation for Romance

Before a Mii can even think about a wedding, they must first become friends with their potential partner. While you cannot directly force two Miis to fall in love, you can significantly influence the odds by managing their personalities and daily interactions.

### Personality and Compatibility
Every Mii is assigned one of 16 personality types based on five specific traits: Movement, Speech, Expressiveness, Attitude, and Overall. These personalities are grouped into four main categories: **Easygoing**, **Outgoing**, **Independent**, and **Confident**. While looks and voices are aesthetic, these traits dictate how well Miis get along.

| Category | Sub-types | Key Characteristics |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Easygoing** | Softie, Carer, Optimist, Dreamer | Empathetic, considerate, and positive. |
| **Outgoing** | Charmer, Adventurer, Bubbly, Hot-blooded | Radiant, wit-heavy, and assertive. |
| **Independent** | Free Spirit, Artist, Lone Wolf, Thinker | Creative, self-sufficient, and introspective. |
| **Confident** | Achiever, Visionary, Rogue, Maverick | Diligent, ambitious, and intelligent. |

> **💡 Tip:** To increase the chances of two Miis meeting, try placing their apartments next to each other horizontally. Miis living in adjacent rooms are more likely to visit one another and ask to be introduced.

### Encouraging Friendships
If a Mii has an orange thought bubble, they may ask to meet another Mii. Always encourage these introductions. You can also foster friendship by giving both Miis similar items or gifts (like the same Wii U console or sewing kit), which encourages them to spend time together in their apartments.

## Step 2: The Confession (Becoming Sweethearts)

Once two Miis are friends, one will eventually develop a crush. This is indicated by a **pink heart icon** appearing in their apartment window. This is the first major milestone in our **tomodachi life how to get married guide**.

When you tap the heart, the Mii will confess their feelings and ask for your advice. You can choose their confession style and location.

| Confession Style | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Traditional** | A straightforward, honest declaration of love. |
| **Romantic** | A poetic approach, often involving flowery language. |
| **Desperate** | A high-pressure, emotional plea for affection. |
| **Cute** | A playful and charming way to ask someone out. |
| **Give a Present** | Using a food item or treasure to win them over. |

### Confession Locations
You can choose locations like the Beach, Tower, Park, or School. If the confession is successful, the other Mii will accept, and their status will change to "Sweetheart" (US) or "Special Someone" (EU). If another Mii interrupts the confession to declare their own love, the target Mii will choose between the two—or reject both.

## Step 3: Strengthening the Relationship

Miis cannot get married immediately after they start dating. Their relationship level must progress to a high enough stage. You can check this by looking at the Mii’s relationship profile; you are looking for phrases like "Greatest Love" or "Let's get married!" (US version).

To speed up this process:
1.  **Travel Tickets:** Give a Mii a Travel Ticket. They will almost always take their sweetheart on vacation, which provides a massive boost to their relationship status.
2.  **Daily Check-ins:** Address any problems (black or green bubbles) the couple has. If they have a fight, resolve it immediately to prevent the relationship from souring.
3.  **Compatibility Tester:** While primarily based on birthdays, use this to see which days have the highest "Romance" luck for your couple.

## Step 4: Mastering the Proposal Mini-Game

When the relationship reaches its peak, a pink heart will appear again. This time, the Mii will announce they want to propose. This triggers the most interactive part of the **tomodachi life how to get married guide**: the proposal mini-game.

### Choosing the Venue
You will be asked to select one of four locations. Each has unique "interruptions" that can make the mini-game more difficult.

| Location | Celebration Style | Common Interruptions |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fireworks Show** | Heart-shaped fireworks | Loud explosions, other attendees shouting. |
| **Train Station** | Crowd applause | Passing trains, PA announcements, coughing. |
| **Amusement Park** | Romantic music | Loud rides, screaming guests. |
| **Fancy Restaurant** | Intimate cheering | Waiters dropping plates, other diners. |

### How to Win the Mini-Game
The goal is to tap the **"NOW!"** button on the touch screen when the sweetheart is thinking about the proposer.
*   A thought bubble will appear over the sweetheart's head with cycling images (food, other Miis, hobbies).
*   Wait until the **proposer's face** appears in the bubble.
*   Tap "NOW!" quickly. You must succeed four times to complete the proposal.
*   You have three "lives" (represented by hearts). If you tap at the wrong time or an interruption occurs, a heart breaks.

> **⚠️ Warning:** If you fail the proposal mini-game three times, the Mii will not become sad, but the proposal will fail. You will have to wait for the heart icon to appear again to retry.

## Step 5: The Wedding and Beyond

Once the proposal is accepted, the wedding happens immediately. If the Miis are children, the game will provide an **Age-O-Matic** to turn them into adults before the ceremony.

### The Ceremony
The wedding features a tuxedo-clad groom and a bride in a white gown. They exchange vows, cut a cake, and the bride tosses her bouquet to the island's other residents. After the ceremony, you will view a slideshow of their relationship highlights while the credits roll.

### Married Life Features
*   **Mii Homes:** The couple will move into a house in the "Mii Homes" section of the island. They still keep their apartments for you to visit them, but they spend most of their time together in the house.
*   **Souvenirs:** After their honeymoon (which happens right after the wedding), they will bring you a unique treasure.
*   **Babies:** If you have the "Baby" setting turned on in the Town Hall, a married couple will eventually ask if they can have a child. This usually happens a few days or weeks after the wedding, provided their relationship remains strong.

## FAQ

**Q: Can Miis of the same gender get married in Tomodachi Life?**

A: In the original versions of Tomodachi Life, marriage is only possible between male and female Miis. While players have found creative workarounds by changing a Mii's "gender" setting while keeping their appearance the same, the game's engine does not natively support same-sex marriage.

**Q: What happens if a Mii rejects a proposal?**

A: In the proposal mini-game, if you lose all three hearts, the sweetheart will ask to leave. However, unlike a failed confession, the Mii who proposed doesn't usually gain a "Sadness" bar. They can try again whenever the pink heart icon reappears.

**Q: Can married Miis get a divorce?**

A: Yes. If a married couple stops getting along and their relationship status drops to a teal or "Not getting along" color, they may stop spending time together. Eventually, one Mii may ask to break up or divorce. If there is currently a baby in the house, they cannot divorce until the baby is fully grown.

**Q: How do I get the "Age-O-Matic" for my child Miis to marry?**

A: You don't need to find one! If two child Miis successfully complete a proposal, the game will automatically prompt you to use an Age-O-Matic. It is provided for free as part of the wedding sequence to ensure they are adults for the ceremony.
