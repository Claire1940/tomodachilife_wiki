## Troubleshooting checklist for common problems

Use this when your setup launches but feels unstable.

| Symptom | Likely Cause | Fast Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Gray/incorrect textures | Backend mismatch | Switch to **Vulkan**, restart emulator |
| Sudden stutters in new areas | Shader compilation | Keep shader cache enabled; play through once |
| Crashes after update | Driver or cache conflict | Update GPU driver; clear/rebuild cache |
| FPS unstable with browser open | RAM pressure / background load | Close heavy apps or upgrade to 16 GB+ |
| Audio crackle during spikes | CPU frametime drops | Lower scaling, cap background tasks |

### Advanced stability tips
- Keep GPU drivers current, but avoid installing every optional utility.
- Use Windows “High Performance” or equivalent custom power plan.
- Exclude emulator folders from aggressive antivirus real-time scanning if you trust the files.
- Avoid stacking multiple overlays (Discord + GeForce + MSI + browser pop-outs).

> **⚠️ Warning:** Don’t benchmark performance in the first 2–3 minutes only. Initial shader compilation can make results look worse than long-session performance.
