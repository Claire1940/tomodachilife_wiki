## Island Life and Daily Activities

Once your Miis are moved into their apartments, the real fun begins. You act as the overseer of a "small island floating in the sea," responsible for the well-being and social lives of your residents. The daily loop involves checking in on your Miis, solving their problems, and watching their relationships evolve in real-time.

### Managing Hunger and Moods

Miis are needy creatures. They will frequently signal you when they are hungry or bored. Feeding them their favorite foods provides a massive boost to their happiness, while giving them something they hate might result in a hilarious (and dramatic) reaction. The 2026 version introduces a wider variety of international cuisines, reflecting a more global island community.
