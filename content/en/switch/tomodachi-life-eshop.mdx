<Section icon={Map} title="Tomodachi Life on the Original 3DS eShop">

The Nintendo 3DS eShop was the primary digital storefront for purchasing *Tomodachi Life* for its original platform. However, the digital landscape for the 3DS has changed significantly over the years. Nintendo officially ceased eShop operations for the 3DS family of systems on March 27, 2023. This means that new digital purchases of *Tomodachi Life* for the 3DS are no longer possible through the eShop.

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Notice">
As of March 27, 2023, the Nintendo 3DS eShop has closed. This means you can no longer purchase **Tomodachi Life** digitally for the 3DS through official channels. Ensure you explore physical copies or the upcoming Switch version.
</Callout>

While the eShop is closed, players who previously purchased *Tomodachi Life* digitally on their 3DS can still redownload the game if they linked their Nintendo Network ID. This ensures that existing owners retain access to their digital library. For those looking to experience the original game for the first time, physical cartridge copies remain the only official avenue, though their availability and price can vary significantly in the secondary market.
