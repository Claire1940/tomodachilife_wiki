## Troubleshooting Common Import Issues

Sometimes, the **mod tomodachi life 2** import process doesn't go as planned. If you find that a Mii isn't appearing or looks "broken" (e.g., missing facial features), it is likely due to a version mismatch or the use of unsupported face paint layers.

*   **Mii won't import from system:** Check if the Mii was created using "Switch-exclusive" assets. If so, the 3DS-based Tomodachi engine cannot render them.
*   **QR Code not scanning:** Ensure the brightness on your screen is high and the camera is focused. Some modded QR codes require a specific region setting on your console.
*   **Mod features not appearing:** Verify that your "Living the Dream" files are in the correct directory on your SD card (typically `luma/titles/`).
