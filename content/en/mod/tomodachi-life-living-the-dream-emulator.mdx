## FAQ

**Q: Is it safe to use custom textures with a tomodachi life living the dream emulator?**

A: Yes, as long as you use established, community-vetted tools and always maintain a backup of your original save files. Never overwrite files without having a safety copy stored in a separate folder.

**Q: Why does my game look "gray" or washed out?**

A: This is almost always caused by running the emulator on the OpenGL backend. Switching your graphics backend to Vulkan in the emulator settings will restore proper texture rendering.

**Q: Can I transfer my original console save to the emulator?**

A: Yes, you can typically export your save file from your hardware and place it into the emulator's user save directory. Ensure the file structure matches the expected format for your specific emulator version.

**Q: Do I need a high-end PC to run this?**

A: *Tomodachi Life* is not a graphically demanding title. Most modern mid-range PCs or laptops from the last few years should run it at full speed without significant issues.
