## Troubleshooting Common Issues

Even with the best hardware, emulating a specialized title like Tomodachi Life can present hurdles. Most issues are related to the "Save Data" structure or the "Mii Maker" integration.

### Mii Face Glitch
If your Miis have no eyes or mouths, the emulator is failing to find the system font and shared data. You must dump the `shared_font.bin` from a 3DS console and place it in the emulator's system folder.

### Voice Synthesis Failure
Since Tomodachi Life uses a proprietary voice engine, the **tomodachi life living the dream Android emulator** must be set to "HLE Audio" or "LLE Audio" depending on the specific build. If the voices sound robotic or high-pitched, try toggling the "Audio Stretching" option in the settings menu.

### Game Crashes at the Fountain
Crashes during the morning market or fountain events are usually caused by a corrupted shader cache. Clearing the cache in the emulator settings and restarting the game typically resolves this.
