<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering Relationship Ladders">

Relationships in *Tomodachi Life* are not static; they fluctuate based on your interventions, daily interactions, and the compatibility of the involved Miis. To manage your island successfully, you must track where your Miis sit on the relationship ladder.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Interaction">
    Miis start as acquaintances. Regular interactions like feeding, gifting, and solving problems increase their bond.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Strengthening Bonds">
    Once bonds reach "Great Pal" or "Best Bud," Miis may seek your advice on whether they should become best friends.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Romance and Marriage">
    Opposite-gender pairs can progress to sweethearts, leading to confessions, dates, and eventually, marriage proposals.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Family Growth">
    After marriage, the couple can move into Mii Homes and potentially request to have a child, starting the parenting cycle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Relationship Risk">
Rejected confessions or unresolved arguments can cause a significant drop in relationship status, potentially leading to breakups or divorce. Always prioritize resolving negative feelings.
</Callout>
