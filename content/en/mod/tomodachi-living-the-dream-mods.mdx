## Troubleshooting Common Issues

Modding is rarely a perfect science. You may encounter issues where a Mii looks different than expected or a mod causes the game to crash.

1.  **Mii Appearance Issues:** If an imported Mii looks "broken," it is likely because it used assets (like specific Miitopia makeup) that the base game cannot read. Try to find a "Vanilla" version of that Mii.
2.  **Save Corruption:** If your game fails to load after installing a mod, remove the most recently added files. This is why backups are vital.
3.  **Access Key Errors:** Access keys for Miitopia can expire if the creator deletes their save or goes offline. Always look for spreadsheets updated in 2026 for the most reliable codes.
