The heart of the experience remains the Miis themselves. In this version, you can add up to 70 distinct characters to your island. Miis will go about their daily lives independently. They will eat, sleep, play games, and most importantly, talk to one another. You will often see them:

*   **Getting into Fights:** Not everyone gets along. You may need to mediate disputes when Miis have a falling out.

## Managing Your Island Residents

As the "lookout" for the island, your primary job is to solve problems. Miis will frequently have a thought bubble over their head (or a green icon if they want to play a game). Solving these problems rewards you with money and increases the Mii's happiness level.

### Daily Routine and Check-ins
The game operates on a real-time clock. Events happen throughout the day, such as morning markets, afternoon magic shows, and evening fireworks. Because the game is passive, the best strategy is to check in for 20-30 minutes, three times a day. This ensures you catch all the unique interactions without burning out on the gameplay loop.

> **⚠️ Warning:** If you ignore your Miis for too long, they won't die, but they might become lonely or have unresolved fights that stunt their relationship growth.
