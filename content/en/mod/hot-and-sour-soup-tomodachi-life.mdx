In **Tomodachi Life: Living the Dream**, the shop has been updated with a cleaner interface, but the "Daily Special" system remains the most effective way to find specific items like Hot and Sour Soup.

*   **Daily Specials**: Every morning, the shop features 5 random items you haven't discovered yet.
*   **Permanent Stock**: Once you buy an item, it is added to your permanent catalog, allowing you to buy it again at any time.

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Find Hot and Sour Soup: Step-by-Step">

If you haven't unlocked the soup yet, follow these steps to maximize your chances of it appearing in the Food Mart. Remember that the game's RNG (Random Number Generation) determines the daily stock, so patience is required.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Check Daily Specials">
    Visit the Food Mart every day immediately after 6:00 AM. Look at the "Daily Specials" tab. Since the soup is an "Uncommon" Main Dish, it has a higher chance of appearing if you have already purchased most common items like Bread or Apples.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Solve Mii Problems">
    Miis often reward you with food items after you solve their problems (represented by black bubbles). High-level Miis or those with "Independent" personalities are more likely to give rare or exotic food gifts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Play Tomodachi Quest">
    In the 2026 version, winning the Tomodachi Quest minigame at the Amusement Park grants you a random rare item. Hot and Sour Soup is frequently included in the prize pool for the "Hard Mode" version of the quest.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Check the Morning Market">
    The Morning Market at the Park (held daily from 6:30 AM to 12:00 PM) often sells "Mystery Bags." These bags can contain any food item in the game, including those not currently in your Food Mart rotation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Mystery Bag Odds">
Mystery Bags cost $5.00 but can contain items worth up to $50.00. It is always worth buying the maximum allowed amount each morning to fill out your collection.
</Callout>
