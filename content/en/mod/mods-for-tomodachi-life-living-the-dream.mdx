## How to Install Mods Safely

Installing **mods for tomodachi life living the dream** requires a specific directory structure on your SD card. Most mods are distributed as a folder containing a "romfs" subfolder.

1.  **Backup Your Save:** Always use a tool like JKSV or Checkpoint to back up your island before installing mods.
2.  **Locate Title ID:** The Title ID for *Tomodachi Life: Living the Dream* is required for the LayeredFS path.
3.  **Transfer Files:** Place the mod files in `atmosphere/contents/[TitleID]/romfs/`.
4.  **Reboot:** Restart your Switch into CFW and launch the game.

> **💡 Tip:** If the game crashes on startup after installing a mod, delete the specific mod folder from your SD card. You do not need to reinstall the entire game.

## FAQ

**Q: Will using mods for Tomodachi Life Living the Dream get my Switch banned?**

A: There is always a risk when using CFW and mods. To minimize this risk, never use mods while connected to Nintendo's official servers (go into Airplane Mode or use DNS blocking). Avoid using mods in any online multiplayer or "Local Play" modes that connect to other users.

**Q: Can I use 3DS Tomodachi Life mods on the Switch version?**

A: No. The file structures and engines are completely different. While you can sometimes port the raw 3D models or textures, they must be converted to the new format used by *Living the Dream*.

**Q: Do I need to update to version 1.0.1 to use mods?**

A: Most modern **mods for tomodachi life living the dream** are being built with version 1.0.1 in mind. While some older mods might work on 1.0.0, the latest patch offers better overall game stability, which helps prevent crashes when multiple mods are active.

**Q: Where can I find the Title ID for the game?**

A: You can find the Title ID by using a file manager on your Switch or by checking community databases. It is a unique 16-character string of numbers and letters specific to your region's version of the game.
