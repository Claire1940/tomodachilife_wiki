2.  **Special Events**: Participate in island events like the "Morning Market" or "Evening Market." Vendors often sell bulk items or exclusive salty snacks that aren't available in the main store.

## FAQ

**Q: Does the Tomodachi Life Living the Dream salty food list change seasonally?**

A: Yes, while many items are available year-round, certain salty snacks like "Roasted Corn" or "Salted Nuts" appear more frequently during the summer and autumn months respectively.

**Q: Can I force a Mii to like a specific salty food?**

A: No, preferences are locked when a Mii is created or when they first taste the item. However, you can use "Wishes" or "Fairy" items in the Living the Dream version to reset a Mii's mood if they have a particularly bad reaction to a meal.

**Q: What happens if I only feed my Miis salty food?**

**Q: Are there any "secret" salty foods in the Living the Dream version?**
