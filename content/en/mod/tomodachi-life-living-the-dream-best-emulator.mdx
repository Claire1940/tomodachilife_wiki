## Resolving Audio and Performance Stutters

One of the most common complaints when using the **tomodachi life living the dream best emulator** is "crackling" audio or micro-stutters during gameplay. This is often caused by the shader cache being rebuilt in real-time or an incorrect audio backend selection.

### Audio Backend Selection
For the best results in 2026, navigate to `Settings > Audio` and set the **Audio Backend** to **OpenAL**. This backend provides lower latency and better spatial positioning for Mii voices compared to SDL2 or Dummy drivers.

### Cache Management
If you encounter persistent stuttering, it is often helpful to clear your old cache files which may be bloated from previous versions of the mod.
*   Right-click the game in your list.
*   Select `Cache Management`.
*   Choose `Purge Shader Cache`.
*   Choose `Purge PPTC Cache`.

> **Tip:** After purging the cache, the game may stutter for the first few minutes of gameplay as it regenerates new shaders. This is normal and will lead to a smoother experience in subsequent sessions.
