## Technical Load: What Affects Performance?

Several in-game activities can put a strain on the system, potentially impacting the perceived **tomodachi life living the dream frame rate**. While the game is well-optimized, certain player-created elements require more resources to render.

1. **Island Expansion:** As you freely expand the land and ocean, the game has to track more geometry and water physics.
2. **Palette House Creations:** Custom-drawn clothing, food, and buildings with high-detail stamps can increase the draw call count.
3. **Mii Density:** Having multiple Miis interacting in a single room (up to eight) increases the animation processing requirements.
4. **Local Wireless:** Using local wireless to trade Miis or items with a friend can occasionally cause minor frame dips during the synchronization process.
