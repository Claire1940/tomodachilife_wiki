## Navigating Gameplay Mechanics

Tomodachi Life is a game of unpredictability. To manage your island effectively, you must understand the core mechanics that drive Mii behavior. The wiki categorizes these into several key areas: Island management, Food preferences, Friendships, and the development of Babies.

### Mii Interaction and Problem Solving

Miis frequently encounter problems, ranging from hunger to social disputes. Solving these problems is the primary way to earn money and level up your islanders.

*   **Hunger:** Every Mii has unique food preferences. Finding their "Super All-Time Favorite" food provides a massive happiness boost.
*   **Friendships:** Miis will ask for advice on making friends or starting romances. Your input directly influences the social web of the island.
*   **Babies:** Once a Mii couple marries and decides to have a child, you will assist in the upbringing until the child is ready to travel via StreetPass or move into their own apartment.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be careful when rejecting a Mii's request to confess their love; it can lead to significant sadness that requires specialized items (like music boxes or travel tickets) to fix.
