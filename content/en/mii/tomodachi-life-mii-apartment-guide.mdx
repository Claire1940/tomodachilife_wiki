## Unlocking Core Island Facilities

The progression of your island is tied directly to the number of Miis you create and the relationships they form. Most facilities unlock automatically once you reach a certain population threshold or solve a specific number of resident problems.

| Facility Name | Unlock Requirement | Primary Function |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fresh Kingdom** | Complete the initial tutorial | Purchase food and snacks for Miis |
| **Wear and Wear** | Create 3 Miis + Solve 1 Request | Buy various clothing and outfits |
| **TNC Renault** | 4 Miis + 2 Friendships established | Purchase and change apartment interiors |
| **Quick Build** | Create 5 Miis | Design and add interactable island parts |
| **News Station** | Create 7 Miis | View daily broadcasts and archived news |
| **Tomoria Restaurant** | Create 8 Miis | Social hub for Miis with exclusive cutscenes |
| **Marketplace** | Create 8 Miis | Buy rotating stock at specific times of day |

> **💡 Tip:** Always prioritize solving the "Orange Bubble" problems first. These often involve making friends, which is a hidden requirement for several late-game building unlocks.

## Advanced Resident Housing and Shared Spaces

As your island evolves in 2026, you will notice that the standard apartment complex isn't the only place your Miis can call home. Relationship dynamics play a massive role in how housing functions.

### The Shared Mii House
One of the most exciting developments in a Mii's life is moving in with someone else. While the main apartment building houses individual Miis, a **Shared Mii House** becomes available under specific conditions. Usually, this occurs when two Miis become best friends or get married. In advanced versions of the game, such as "Living the Dream," up to eight Miis can share a single large apartment, creating a communal living environment that boosts friendship levels significantly.

### Apartment Expansion
The physical size of the Mii Apartment building grows automatically. You do not need to "buy" more floors; rather, as you add more residents via the Mii Maker or QR codes, the building will visually expand to accommodate them. By the time you reach a large population, the tower becomes a landmark visible from almost every other point on the island.

## Specialized Workshops and Economic Facilities

To truly master the **tomodachi life mii apartment guide**, you must look beyond housing and focus on the economy of your island. Collecting treasures and designing custom items provides the funds necessary to buy expensive interiors and rare foods.

### The Right Price Pawn Shop
The Pawn Shop is vital for clearing out your inventory and earning extra gold. To unlock this facility, you must obtain treasures from your Miis. Treasures are typically earned by:
1. Winning mini-games (indicated by a **Green Bubble**).
2. Checking in on Miis while they are dreaming at night.
3. Receiving "thank you" gifts after leveling up a resident.

Even if you lose a mini-game and receive a "pity prize" like a box of tissues, it still counts as a treasure and will trigger the Pawn Shop's construction.

### Pallet House and My Treasures
The Pallet House Workshop is where creativity meets gameplay. Unlocked after creating 7 Miis, this facility allows you to design custom clothing, treasures, and even pets. 

| Creation Category | Unlock Method |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Custom Clothing** | Studio Workshop tab in Pallet House |
| **Island Objects** | Upgrade Wishing Fountain via Warm Fuzzies |
| **Pets** | Create a pet treasure in Pallet House |
| **My Treasures Store** | Create your very first treasure in the Workshop |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Some custom items require specific "Wishes" from the Wishing Fountain. Ensure you are collecting "Warm Fuzzies" by keeping your Miis' happiness levels high to unlock these advanced design options.

## Social Landmarks and Romance

Beyond the walls of the apartment, Miis need places to interact. These landmarks often have the most specific unlock requirements because they depend on the emotional state of your residents.

### The Ferris Wheel
The Ferris Wheel is the ultimate romantic destination. While the requirements can vary based on your island's size, it generally unlocks once a Mii successfully confesses their love to another resident. This location is famous for proposal cutscenes. If you have reached the population requirement but the Ferris Wheel hasn't appeared, focus on fostering a romantic relationship between two Miis with high compatibility.

### PhotoTomo
If you enjoy documenting your island's history, PhotoTomo is essential. This facility unlocks once you have fostered **10 different friendships** on your island. It allows you to take staged photos of your Miis, which can then be saved to your console or shared with friends.

## Maximizing the Wishing Fountain

The Wishing Fountain is a unique facility that rewards long-term play. It is unlocked simply by leveling up a Mii for the first time—an event that happens naturally as you feed them and solve their problems.

**Benefits of the Wishing Fountain in 2026:**
*   **Daily Donations:** Check the fountain every morning to collect pocket change from your residents.
*   **Warm Fuzzies:** As you interact positively with Miis, you collect a currency known as "Warm Fuzzies."
*   **Granting Wishes:** You can spend Warm Fuzzies to unlock new room types, quirks, and even structural upgrades for other buildings.

## FAQ

**Q: How many Miis can live in the main apartment complex?**

A: In the standard version of Tomodachi Life, the apartment can eventually hold up to 100 Miis. The building expands its capacity automatically as you add more residents.

**Q: Why hasn't the Pawn Shop unlocked yet even though I have 10 Miis?**

A: The Pawn Shop is not tied to population count, but to treasures. You must play mini-games with your Miis (look for the green bubbles) or collect items from their dreams to receive a treasure. Once you have at least one treasure in your inventory, the shop will open.

**Q: Can I move a Mii from one apartment to another?**

A: Yes, you can change a Mii's apartment location or interior by visiting the Office or using the TNC Renault facility once it is unlocked. However, Miis usually choose their own neighbors unless you intervene.

**Q: What is the benefit of this tomodachi life mii apartment guide for the "Living the Dream" mod?**

A: This guide covers the specific requirements for "Living the Dream" additions, such as the Shared Mii House (which allows 8 Miis to live together) and the Pallet House design mechanics, which are significantly expanded in that version of the game.

**Q: How do I get more "Warm Fuzzies" for the Wishing Fountain?**

A: Warm Fuzzies are earned by making your Miis happy. Feeding them their favorite foods, giving them gifts they enjoy, and successfully helping them make friends or fall in love are the fastest ways to farm this currency.
