<Section icon={Target} title="Steps to Change Mii Hair Color">
Changing a Mii's hair color in Tomodachi Life is a straightforward process once they initiate the request. Follow these steps to help your Mii achieve their desired new look.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Wait for a Hair Request">
    Your Mii will eventually have a thought bubble indicating they want a "new look" or to "dye their hair." This is your cue to interact with them. Tap on their apartment or the thought bubble to initiate the conversation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Confirm the Desire for a New Color">
    The Mii will express their wish to change their hair. They might say something like, "I'd love to try a new hair color!" or "I feel like a change." Confirm their request to proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Choose a Hair Color Spray">
    You will be presented with a selection of hair color sprays. These typically include a variety of natural and vibrant colors. Scroll through the options and select the color you think best suits your Mii, or the one they hinted at.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Observe the Transformation">
    After selecting a color, the Mii will use the spray, and their hair will instantly change. They will then express their reaction, usually delight or satisfaction with their new appearance. If they're bald, enjoy the humorous outcome!
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Experimentation Encouraged">
Don't be afraid to experiment with different hair colors! If a Mii doesn't like a color, they might ask to change it again later, or you can use the hair salon item to offer them a new style and color.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Hair Color vs. Hairstyle: What's the Difference?">
It's important to distinguish between changing a Mii's hair color and their hairstyle. While both contribute to a Mii's overall look, they are separate customization options.

| Feature     | Description                                     | How to Change                                  | Cost/Requirement                               |
| :---------- | :---------------------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------- |
| **Hair Color** | The shade of the Mii's current hairstyle.       | Mii's request for "dye hair"                   | Free (when requested by Mii)                   |
| **Hairstyle** | The cut and style of the Mii's hair (e.g., short, long, curly, spiked). | Mii's request for "new hairstyle" or Hair Salon item | Free (when requested by Mii), Hair Salon item (requires purchase) |

<Callout type="info" title="Hair Salon Item">
The Hair Salon item, available for purchase at the travel agency, allows you to change a Mii's hairstyle and color at your discretion, without waiting for a request. This offers more control over their appearance.
</Callout>

</Section>
