<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Hair Styling with Pro Mode">
To truly bring Hatsune Miku to life, you'll need to utilize Tomodachi Life's Pro mode for hair customization. This allows for intricate details like hair shadows and highlights, which are essential for her iconic look.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enter Pro Mode">
    After setting the basic hair structure and changing the main hair color to white, switch to "Pro mode" in the Mii Maker.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Fill Bangs with Hair Color">
    Using the fill tool in Pro mode, color in the bangs with Miku's signature turquoise (or a similar vibrant blue-green).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply Hair Shadow">
    Slide the hair color down and right diagonally to create a shadow effect. This adds depth to her hair. Turn on the "Outline" option for cleaner edges.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Create Skin Color Shadow">
    Move the color down and right diagonally again, but this time use a skin tone color to create a subtle shadow under the hair on her forehead. This step requires a bit of artistic touch – undo if it doesn't look right and clean up edges with the eraser.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Outline Inner Hair">
    Color-pick the hair shadow color you used earlier and carefully outline the inner part of her bangs. This defines the shape.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Add Hair Highlight">
    Drag a lighter shade of her hair color diagonally left and up to create highlights. Outline the outer part of these highlights to make them pop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="7" title="Duplicate and Adjust Bangs">
    Duplicate the left side of the bangs. Mirror it and carefully adjust its placement to create her symmetrical, iconic look. This may require some fine-tuning.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Achieving the Look">
The key to Pro mode is layering. Think of it like painting: start with base colors, then add shadows, and finally highlights to create a three-dimensional effect.
</Callout>

</Section>
