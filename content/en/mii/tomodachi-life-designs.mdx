<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Tomodachi Life Designs: Mii Customization">

Creating memorable characters is the foundation of any great island. With the right **tomodachi life designs**, you can transform simple avatars into highly detailed replicas of anime characters, video game heroes, and real-life celebrities. The secret lies in looking at facial features not just as they are labeled, but as shapes that can be resized, rotated, and layered. 

For instance, eyebrows can be rotated and lowered to act as eyelids or custom eye creases. Mustaches and beards can be repositioned to serve as chin shadows, face paint, or unique jawlines. By layering these elements, you can bypass the standard limitations of the Mii Maker and achieve a level of detail that looks incredibly realistic.

| Mii Design Category | Key Feature Focus | Customization Technique | Recommended Personality Type |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |
| **Anime Characters** | Oversized eyes, colorful hair | Position eyebrows beneath eyes to create dramatic lower lashes. | Outgoing / Trendsetter |
| **Retro Game Icons** | Blocky, pixelated features | Use thick, square eyebrows and glasses to simulate 8-bit sprites. | Independent / Artist |
| **Gothic / Alt Styles** | Dark makeup, pale skin | Layer round nose shapes and dark glasses to create custom eyeshadow. | Confident / Brainiac |
| **Realistic Portraits** | Subtle shading, accurate proportions | Use small mustaches shifted down to create realistic chin dimples. | Easygoing / Softie |

To make your characters truly stand out, pay close attention to the spacing between the eyes and the nose. Lowering the entire face structure slightly on the head template often gives the Mii a more youthful, expressive appearance. Additionally, do not hesitate to use the hair dye spray to match specific color palettes that are not available in the default Mii creator menu.

<Callout type="success" title="Styling Success">
When designing custom Miis, always test their facial expressions in the Mii Maker preview. A feature placement that looks perfect when neutral might distort strangely during animations if the eyes or eyebrows are placed too close to the outer boundaries of the face.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Step-by-Step Custom QR Code Sharing Guide">

Sharing and importing **tomodachi life designs** is incredibly easy thanks to the QR code system. This feature allows you to scan designs created by players worldwide, bringing unique outfits, rare items, and perfectly sculpted Miis directly to your game without having to recreate them from scratch.

Follow these steps to import custom designs or export your own creations to share with the community.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Town Hall">
    Enter the Town Hall from your main island map. This serves as the administrative hub for all Mii management and sharing features.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select the QR Code Option">
    Tap on the QR Code button on the touch screen. You will be presented with two choices: "Scan QR Code" or "Create QR Code."
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Align the Camera">
    If scanning, point your console's outer camera at the QR code image on your computer screen or phone. Ensure the code fits entirely within the on-screen guide frame.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm and Save">
    Once the camera recognizes the code, the Mii or outfit design will appear. Review the character details and select "Save" to add them to your apartments.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Import Restrictions">
Some QR codes are region-locked depending on the version of the game they were created in (North America, Europe, or Japan). If a code fails to scan, verify that the source matches your game's region.
</Callout>

</Section>
