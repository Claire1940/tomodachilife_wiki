<Section icon={Trophy} title="Unlocking Advanced Expressions">

As you play, you can expand the "vocabulary" of your Mii's expressions by unlocking new items and facilities. The **Tomodachi Life expression generator** isn't static; it grows as the island develops.

1.  **Concert Hall**: Unlock this by giving a Mii a "Song" after they level up. This allows them to express themselves through various musical genres like Rock, Pop, or Opera.
2.  **Gifts**: Items like the Wii U, Sewing Machine, or Disposable Camera unlock new idle "expression" animations in the Mii's apartment.
3.  **Interiors**: A Mii's room is a static expression of their personality. Matching an "Artist" Mii with a "Creative" interior increases their happiness generation rate.
