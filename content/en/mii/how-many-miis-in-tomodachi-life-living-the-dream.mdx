## Importing and Sharing Miis

A major point of discussion among fans is the restriction on Mii sharing. Unlike the 3DS version, which used QR codes, *Living the Dream* does not have a native "Share Mii" function within the game itself. This is reportedly a safety measure implemented by Nintendo to prevent the distribution of unfiltered content.

However, there is a reliable loophole using **Miitopia** (either the full game or the free eShop demo). Follow these steps to bring your favorite characters into the game:

1.  Open **Miitopia** on your Nintendo Switch.
2.  Navigate to the "Mii Characters" menu.
3.  Select a Mii and choose "To Nintendo Switch System."
4.  Launch *Tomodachi Life: Living the Dream*.
5.  When creating a new resident, select "Create based on a Mii on your system."

> **⚠️ Warning:** When importing Miis from Miitopia, specialized makeup and wigs will not be copied over. You will need to use the new in-game face paint editor to recreate complex looks.

## FAQ

**Q: Exactly how many miis in tomodachi life living the dream can I have?**

**Q: Can I transfer my Miis from the 3DS version of Tomodachi Life?**

A: No. Most core facilities and terraforming tools are unlocked by the time you reach 6 to 10 residents. The remaining slots are for your personal enjoyment and to see more varied social interactions.
