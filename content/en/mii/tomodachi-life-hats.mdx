<Section icon={Target} title="Acquiring Hats: Your Shopping Guide">
There are several primary methods to obtain **Tomodachi Life hats** for your Miis. The most common is through the Hat Shop, but special events and gifts also play a significant role. Understanding these avenues will ensure your Miis never go without stylish headwear.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Hat Shop">
    This is your primary source for most hats. The stock rotates daily, offering a variety of **standard and unique hats**. Check back frequently for new arrivals.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Special Events & Travelers">
    Occasionally, special events on your island or visiting **traveling Miis** may offer rare or exclusive hats not found in the shop. Participate in these events to expand your collection.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gifts from Miis">
    As your Miis grow closer to each other or to you, they might **gift hats** they've found or made. These can sometimes be unique to the gifting Mii's personality.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="StreetPass & SpotPass">
    Utilize **StreetPass and SpotPass** features to receive special items, including rare hats, from other players or Nintendo distributions. This is a great way to get exclusive headwear.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Limited Stock Alert">

</Callout>
