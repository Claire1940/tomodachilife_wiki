When building your characters, you are essentially mixing five core traits: Movement, Speech, Energy, Thinking, and Vibe. These combinations result in one of 16 personality types, each represented by a specific color that influences the character's starting outfit and room decor.

| Trait | Description | Impact on Mii |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Movement** | Ranges from Slow to Quick | Affects walking animations and mini-game speed |
| **Speech** | Ranges from Polite to Direct | Determines how they interact with other Miis |
| **Energy** | Ranges from Low to High | Influences how often they want to play games |
| **Thinking** | Ranges from Logical to Dreamy | Affects the types of gifts they prefer |
| **Vibe** | Ranges from Serious to Quirky | Dictates their overall emotional response |

<Section icon={Heart} title="Relationship Dynamics and Player Agency">

One of the biggest changes in 2026 is the increased player agency regarding relationships. While you still cannot "force" a marriage like in The Sims, you can give your **tomodachi life characters** a significant nudge. You can now introduce Miis to each other and suggest conversation topics to help them bond.

**Relationship Stages:**
1.  **Strangers**: No interaction history.
2.  **Acquaintances**: Have met once or twice.
3.  **Friends**: Regularly hang out in rooms or the cafe.
4.  **Best Friends**: Highest friendship tier; rarely fight.
5.  **Crushes**: One-sided or mutual romantic interest.
6.  **Married**: Miis move into a shared home together.

<Callout type="success" title="Success Strategy">
If two Miis are butting heads, try gifting them the same hobby item (like a guitar). Sharing an activity increases the chance of them resolving their conflict and becoming friends again.
</Callout>

Relationships can evolve or devolve over time. Even a happily married couple can face a divorce if their compatibility scores drop or if they have too many unresolved arguments. Watching these stories unfold is the core appeal of the Tomodachi experience.

**The Time Travel Penalty**
Nintendo has implemented strict anti-time-travel measures. If you change your system clock, you will be penalized with a **24-hour lockout**. During this time:
- Daily specials will not refresh.
- Seasonal events will be paused.
- Donations at the fountain will stop.

<Callout type="warning" title="Nighttime Activities">
Don't ignore the game at night! While Miis sleep, you can enter their dreams to find unique treasures that aren't available in the shops.
