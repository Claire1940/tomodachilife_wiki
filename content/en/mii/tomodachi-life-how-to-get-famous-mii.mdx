## Using the "Re-Rolling" Trick for Rapid Growth

For players who are impatient, the "re-rolling" technique is a tried-and-true method to force social events. This involves saving the game, checking for social bubbles (orange icons), and if the desired Mii isn't interacting, exiting without saving to try again.

1.  **Save your game** while looking at the map.
2.  **Check the Apartments.** Look for orange "friendship" bubbles or pink "romance" bubbles.
3.  **If the "Famous" Mii has no bubble,** press the Home button and close the software.
4.  **Reload the game.** The internal clock and RNG (random number generator) will reset, potentially giving your target Mii a new social opportunity.

> **💡 Tip:** This method is also highly effective for forcing "Best Friend" status. If two Miis are already friends, keep re-rolling until one asks to become "Best Friends" with the other.
