2.  **Select QR Code:** Tap the "QR Code" button located in the menu.
3.  **Scan Image:** Choose "Scan QR Code." This will activate the 3DS outer cameras.
4.  **Align the Frame:** Position the QR code within the on-screen brackets. The game will automatically recognize the data and prompt you to save the new resident.
5.  **Finalize Details:** Once the **tomodachi life Mii QR import** is successful, you can still edit the Mii's voice and personality if you wish to customize them further.

## Troubleshooting QR Import Issues

Sometimes, a **tomodachi life Mii QR import** fails. This can happen for several reasons, ranging from hardware limitations to file corruption.

1.  **Screen Glare:** If you are scanning from a phone or monitor, the 3DS camera might struggle with the light. Lower the brightness of the source screen.
2.  **Distance:** Hold the 3DS about 8-12 inches away from the code. Moving too close can cause the camera to lose focus.
3.  **Region Restrictions:** As mentioned, some Miis are tied to specific game regions. If you receive an error saying "This QR Code cannot be read," it is likely a region mismatch.
4.  **Modified Miis:** If a Mii was created using external save editors, the QR code might contain "illegal" parts that the standard game cannot process.

**Q: Are Mii QR codes from the original Wii compatible?**

**Q: Where is the best place to find new Mii QR codes in 2026?**
