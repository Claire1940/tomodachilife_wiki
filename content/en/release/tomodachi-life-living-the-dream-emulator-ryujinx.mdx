### Graphics Settings Configuration
1.  **Graphics Backend**: Select **Vulkan** for the best performance on most modern NVIDIA and AMD cards. OpenGL may still be useful for older Intel integrated graphics, but it generally offers lower frame rates.
2.  **Resolution Scale**: For most users, **2x (1440p/2160p)** provides a sharp look without taxing the GPU. If you have a high-end card, 3x or 4x scale can make the Mii models look incredibly smooth.
3.  **Anisotropic Filtering**: Set this to **Auto** or **16x** to ensure textures on the island floor and walls remain crisp at a distance.
4.  **Shader Cache**: Ensure this is **Enabled**. This prevents stuttering the first time you enter a new shop or Mii apartment.

### Audio and Input
Tomodachi Life relies heavily on text-to-speech and quirky audio cues. Ensure your audio backend is set to **SDL2** to avoid any desync between the Mii's mouth movements and their synthesized voices. For input, Ryujinx supports most modern controllers; however, using a controller with a touchpad (like the DualSense) can emulate the original touch-screen controls more naturally for certain mini-games.
