<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Understanding Mii Personalities in Tomodachi Life">
In Tomodachi Life, each Mii on your island is assigned one of 16 distinct personalities, which are determined by four adjustable sliders during their creation. These personalities are crucial as they dictate a Mii's behavior, interactions, preferences, and overall social dynamics within the game. From how they speak and react to food, to how they form friendships and romantic relationships, a Mii's personality shapes their entire island life. Understanding these traits is key to managing your island effectively and fostering the relationships you desire.

The game categorizes these 16 personalities into four main groups: Easygoing, Outgoing, Independent, and Confident. Each group shares a general tone, but individual personalities within them offer unique nuances. For instance, an "Optimist" (Easygoing) will react differently to situations than a "Leader" (Outgoing), despite both being generally positive influences.

### How the Personality Quiz Works
The personality chart is built from four sliders: Movement, Speech, Expressiveness, and Attitude. The game maps these into one of 16 personality results. The "Overall" row is purely cosmetic and does not alter the final personality outcome. A Mii's personality profile becomes visible once your island has at least one resident exhibiting that type.

<Callout type="warning" title="Personality is Permanent">
Once a Mii's personality is set, it cannot be changed. Plan carefully during Mii creation, especially if you have specific relationship goals in mind.
</Callout>

### Personality's Role in Compatibility
Personality groups significantly influence compatibility. Miis with similar personality types generally tend to get along better and form stronger bonds more easily. Conversely, Miis with vastly different personalities might experience more arguments or difficulties in building strong relationships. This makes personality a crucial planning signal for building friendship-heavy or romance-heavy islands.

While the game has its random elements, understanding Mii personalities and compatibility gives you an edge in shaping your island's social landscape. You can influence relationships by offering advice, organizing dates, and providing travel tickets.

### Relationship Ladders and Statuses
Tomodachi Life tracks various relationship categories, each with its own status ladder. These ladders are critical indicators of how Miis are progressing in their social bonds.

| Relationship Category | Strongest Status (NA Version) | Weakest Status (NA Version) | Key Actions to Influence |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Friends, Family, Siblings** | Best Bud | Not Getting Along | Positive advice, resolving arguments, shared activities |
| **Sweetheart** | Let's Get Married! | Kinda Getting Along | Dates, travel tickets, successful confessions |
| **Spouse** | Soul Mate | Very Happy (can still divorce) | Positive interactions, raising children, gifts |
| **Ex-Sweethearts/Ex-Spouses** | Acquaintances (can improve or worsen) | Avoid Each Other | Player intervention, new relationships |

<Callout type="success" title="Boost Relationships">
If a Mii asks for advice about another Mii, telling them they are "great together" can significantly boost their relationship status. Travel tickets also reliably increase relationship levels.
</Callout>

### How Relationships Break Down
Arguments, failed reconciliations, and rejected confessions can quickly damage bonds. If a conversation goes badly, it can turn into an argument, potentially lowering the relationship. Unresolved fights can even lead to Miis being removed from each other's relationship lists. For romantic pairs, an unresolved fight can lead to a breakup, and a rejected confession can drop both sides by as many as three levels.

### Preventing Breakups and Divorce
If a sweetheart asks to break up and you advise them to "work things out," a successful reconciliation resets a negative dating relationship back to its default level. However, this recovery rule does not apply the same way for spouses asking for a divorce; married status does not automatically reset.

<Callout type="info">
Miis marked as related to the real-life player will not date that look-alike Mii and will fight them less often. This is a subtle way to manage certain social outcomes.
</Callout>
