## Strategic Feeding: When to Use Gazpacho

Using **gazpacho tomodachi life** effectively requires a bit of strategy. You don't want to waste food on a Mii who is already full, nor do you want to feed a Mii their favorite food when their happiness bar is already nearly at 100%.

*   **Check Fullness:** A Mii can hold about 100% fullness. Gazpacho takes up a moderate amount of space. If a Mii is at 80% fullness, wait until they are hungrier before feeding them a multi-course meal.
*   **Healing Sadness:** If a Mii has a "broken heart" or has lost a friend, feeding them their favorite foods is one of the quickest ways to heal their sadness. While Gazpacho isn't a guaranteed "cure-all," it helps chip away at the sadness meter.
*   **The "Space" Animation:** If you are recording gameplay or want to see the most dramatic animation in the game, keep a stock of Gazpacho and test it on every new Mii that moves into your apartments.

> **Warning:** Feeding a Mii their "Worst Ever" food will significantly drop their happiness. If they were close to leveling up, they might lose all that progress instantly.
