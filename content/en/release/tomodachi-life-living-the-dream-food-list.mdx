## How to Discover "Super Liked" Foods

Finding a Mii's "Super Liked" or "All-Time Favorite" food is one of the most rewarding aspects of managing your island. When a Mii eats their favorite food, they perform a unique animation—often involving dancing or blasting off into space—and receive a massive boost to their level bar.

To efficiently navigate the **tomodachi life living the dream food list** and find these favorites, follow these steps:

1.  **Observe Requests:** Always prioritize feeding a Mii what they specifically ask for. This bypasses some of the negative "randomness" and helps narrow down their preferred attributes.
2.  **Test by Category:** Instead of feeding random items, try feeding one item from each major category (Meat, Fruit, Bread, etc.) to see which ones consistently get a "Liked" reaction.
3.  **Check the Shop Daily:** The food shop rotates its inventory. Buy at least one of every new item to ensure your "Food Collection" is complete.
4.  **Use the "Soft Reset" Method:** If you are determined to find a favorite, you can save before a feeding session and restart if the reaction is negative. However, as noted by researchers, some preferences are baked into the Mii's "seed" and won't change regardless of how many times you reset.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Feeding a Mii their "Worst Ever" food will cause them to melt into a puddle of despair. This significantly lowers their happiness and should be avoided unless you are trying to see every animation in the game.

## FAQ

**Q: Can a Mii's favorite food change over time?**

A: No, a Mii's "All-Time Favorite" and "Worst Ever" foods are fixed from the moment the Mii is created. They are tied to the Mii's internal data and will remain the same throughout their life on the island.

**Q: Why does my Mii hate a food that they previously liked?**

A: This usually happens if you feed the Mii the same item too many times in a short period. Miis crave variety. If you only feed them sandwiches, they will eventually grow tired of them, and the happiness boost will decrease.

**Q: Is there a way to see the full tomodachi life living the dream food list in-game?**

A: You can view your "Collection" menu to see every food item you have ever purchased or received. Items you haven't found yet will appear as question marks.

**Q: Does the Mii's personality affect what they like?**
