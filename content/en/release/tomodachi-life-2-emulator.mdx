## Troubleshooting Common Emulator Issues

Even with the best setup, you might encounter technical hitches. Here are the most common solutions for the **tomodachi life 2 emulator** community in 2026.

### Game Crashes on Boot
This is almost always caused by using the "Mainline" build of Ryujinx instead of the **Canary** build. The Canary build includes experimental fixes for the virtual memory management used by Tomodachi Life mods. Ensure your software version matches the requirements listed in the [Official Ryujinx Documentation](https://ryujinx.org/).

### Missing Mii Faces
If your Miis appear with blank faces or missing features, it is likely a firmware issue. Re-install the system firmware and ensure you have the "Mii Component" files included in your system directory. Without proper firmware, the emulator cannot render the unique facial structures that make the game famous.

### Audio Stuttering
Audio issues are usually tied to the "Frame Management" settings. Navigate to the **System** tab in settings and ensure **VSync** is turned on. If the game runs too fast (Turbo Mode), the audio will distort. Keep the "Game Speed" at 100% unless you are intentionally trying to skip long cutscenes.
