<Section icon={Target} title="Unlocking and Using the Compatibility Tester">

To truly master **친모아 성격 궁합**, you must unlock the Compatibility Tester. This facility provides a numerical percentage and a weather-based forecast for any two Miis on your island. While it doesn't guarantee a specific outcome, it shows the "raw" potential of a pairing based on their birthdays and personality traits.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Meet the Unlock Requirements">
    You must have at least 2 male Miis and 2 female Miis living on the island. Additionally, you must solve at least 30 Mii problems (black or orange bubbles).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Check Birthdays">
    The tester requires full birthdays (Month/Day/Year) for both Miis. If a Mii's birthday is missing, the compatibility cannot be calculated.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select the Pairing">
    Choose two Miis to test. The game will provide a Friendship forecast (same gender or different age groups) or a Romance forecast (opposite gender, same age group).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Interpret the Results">
    Results are shown as a percentage (0% to 100%) and a weekly forecast. A "Sunny" forecast indicates a great week for bonding, while a "Stormy" forecast suggests potential arguments.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Forecast Icon | Meaning | Compatibility Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sunny** | Excellent | High chance of successful confessions |
| **Cloudy** | Average | Steady relationship with minor growth |
| **Rainy** | Poor | Likely to have misunderstandings |
| **Stormy** | Terrible | High risk of major fights or breakups |

<Callout type="warning" title="Tester Limitation">
The Compatibility Tester is a forecast, not a destiny. A 10% compatibility couple can still get married if you intervene with the right advice and gifts!
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="The Relationship Status Ladder">

In Tomodachi Life and the 2026 *Living the Dream* edition, relationships are tracked through specific "status labels." These labels appear in the Mii's profile and change based on their daily interactions. Knowing these levels is essential for predicting when a Mii might ask to propose or when they are at risk of ending a friendship.

| Category | High Status (Green) | Mid Status (Orange) | Low Status (Purple) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Friendship** | Best Bud / BFF | Great Pal / Good Pal | Not Getting Along |
| **Romance** | Let's Get Married! | Super in Love | Unhappy |
| **Marriage** | Soul Mate | Super Happy | Not Happy |

**Friendship Status Levels (North America):**
1. **Best Bud**: The highest level for standard friends.
2. **Good Buddy**: A very strong, stable bond.
3. **Great Pal**: A reliable friendship.
4. **Good Pal**: The default positive status.
5. **Getting Along OK**: Neutral territory.
6. **Kinda Getting Along**: Starting to drift apart.
7. **Not Getting Along**: High risk of the Mii wanting to stop being friends.

<Callout type="success" title="Romance Signal">
If a Sweetheart relationship reaches 'Let's Get Married!' or 'Super in Love', a proposal request is likely to appear within the next few gameplay sessions.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Marriage, Breakups, and Divorce">

The ultimate goal of **친모아 성격 궁합** for many is marriage and starting a family. Once a couple is married, they move into Mii Homes. However, the game remains a simulation, meaning relationships still require maintenance. If a marriage status drops into the purple 'Not Happy' zone, the couple may ask for a divorce, which removes them from Mii Homes and returns them to the Apartments.

**The Path to Mii Homes:**
1. **Confession**: One Mii asks the other to be their Sweetheart.
2. **Dating**: The couple spends time together, raising their romance status.
3. **Proposal**: A Mii asks for your help to propose via a timing minigame.
4. **Wedding**: A ceremony is held, and the couple goes on a honeymoon.
5. **Mii Homes**: The couple moves into a permanent house on the island.

<Callout type="warning" title="Divorce Warning">
A divorce is permanent in Tomodachi Life. While the Miis can technically date again later, their history as 'Ex-Spouse' makes the compatibility much harder to rebuild.
</Callout>

| Event | Requirement | Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Proposal** | High Romance Status | Marriage & Mii Home unlock |
| **Baby Request** | Married + Positive Status | A child arrives in 1-2 days |
| **Breakup** | Low Romance Status | Miis become 'Ex-Sweethearts' |
| **Divorce** | Low Marriage Status | Miis move back to Apartments |
