## Daily Income Sources and Events

The most consistent way to maintain your funds is to participate in the scheduled daily events around the island. These activities are low-effort and provide a reliable baseline of income every single day.

### The Fountain Donation
Every day, your Miis will gather at the Fountain for a donation event. The amount of money collected depends on the number of Miis living on your island and their overall happiness levels. 

| Event | Time of Day | Potential Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Morning Market** | 6:30 AM - 12:00 PM | Discounted items for resale |
| **Fountain Donations** | All Day (Once daily) | Variable based on population |
| **Afternoon Market** | 12:00 PM - 9:00 PM | Food and clothing deals |
| **Night Market** | 9:00 PM - 6:30 AM | Rare mystery bags |

> **💡 Tip:** Always check the Fountain first thing in the morning. A full island of 100 Miis can provide a significant daily boost that covers basic food costs for the entire day.

## Solving Mii Problems for Profit

The primary loop of the game revolves around the "bubbles" that appear in apartment windows. Solving these problems is the most active way to earn money. Each solved problem rewards you with a small amount of cash and experience for the Mii.

### Problem Types and Payouts
Not all problems are created equal. Some require items you must purchase, while others only require a simple interaction. 

1.  **Hunger (Orange Bubble):** Feed the Mii. Their favorite foods provide more experience but the cash reward remains relatively stable.
2.  **Social Issues (Pink/Orange Bubble):** Helping Miis make friends or resolve fights. These often result in larger "thank you" gifts.
3.  **Needs (Green Bubble):** Giving a Mii a bath set, a swing, or a new outfit.
4.  **Games (Green Bubble with Game Controller):** Playing a mini-game. Winning these is crucial because they provide treasures.

| Problem Level | Cash Reward | Bonus Item |
|---|---|---|
| **Simple (Food/Clothes)** | $5.00 - $20.00 | None |
| **Level Up Reward** | $0.00 | Silver/Gold Coin or Interior |
| **Friendship Success** | $10.00 - $30.00 | Pocket Tissue or Treasure |
| **Conflict Resolution** | $20.00 - $50.00 | Rare Treasure |

## Leveling Up Miis for Financial Gain

Every time a Mii's happiness meter fills up, they level up. While the level-up screen usually asks you to give the Mii a gift (like a song or a tool), the process itself triggers a financial reward. 

When a Mii reaches a new level, you are often gifted a "Silver Coin" or a "Gold Coin" if their happiness was significantly boosted by a favorite food. These coins can be sold at the Pawn Shop for $100 and $200 respectively. In the *Living the Dream* mod, certain milestones also grant unique furniture sets that can be sold for high prices if you prefer the cash over the aesthetics.

## Mini-Game Mastery

Winning mini-games is the only way to get treasures without spending money. To maximize your income, you must become proficient at the following games:

*   **The Catch Game:** Focus on the shadow of the item rather than the item itself. Dropped items result in "Pocket Tissues" (worth $1), while caught items are usually worth $10 to $50.
*   **Mii Quiz:** Pay attention to the silhouettes and the zoomed-in photos. These are the fastest games to complete.
*   **Match:** A standard memory game. Use your phone to take a quick photo of the grid if you are struggling to remember the placements.

A: You cannot "lose" money through penalties, but you can waste it by buying expensive food that a Mii hates. If a Mii has a "Worst Ever" reaction to a food item, you get very little experience and no financial return for the investment.

**Q: Are there any cheats for money in the Living the Dream mod?**
