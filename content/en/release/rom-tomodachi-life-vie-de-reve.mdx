<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Personality Groups and Traits">

The heart of the **rom tomodachi life vie de reve** experience is the personality system. During Mii creation, four sliders—Movement, Speech, Expressiveness, and Attitude—determine which of the 16 types your Mii falls into. These are divided into four main temperaments.

| Personality Group | Types Included | General Tone | Social Behavior |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Easygoing** | Softie, Optimist, Buddy, Dreamer | Relaxed and empathetic | High patience, rarely fights |
| **Outgoing** | Trendsetter, Entertainer, Charmer, Leader | Social and energetic | Loves attention, makes friends fast |
| **Independent** | Free Spirit, Artist, Lone Wolf, Thinker | Self-sufficient and creative | Prefers quiet activities, private |
| **Confident** | Designer, Adventurer, Brainiac, Go-Getter | Driven and organized | Assertive, focused on goals |

<Callout type="info" title="Personality Influence">
Personalities don't just change dialogue; they affect which Miis are likely to become Best Friends. Miis in the same group (e.g., two Easygoing types) generally have a higher baseline compatibility score.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Importing Miis via QR Codes">

Populating your island is much faster when using the QR code system. You can scan codes from the original 3DS version or new Switch-native codes to bring celebrities, anime characters, and friends into your game instantly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Town Hall">
    Go to the **Town Hall** building on your main island map. This is the central hub for all administrative tasks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select QR Code Options">
    Choose the **QR Code** menu. Here you can choose to either "Scan QR Code" or "Check My QR Code" to share your own residents.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Align the Camera">
    Point your Switch camera (or use the handheld mode) at the QR code. Ensure the lighting is bright enough for the sensor to pick up the data patterns.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Finalize Profile">
    Once scanned, you must confirm the Mii's name, creator info, and relationship to the player. Note that happiness levels and special items do not transfer via QR.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Data Reset">
Importing a Mii via QR code will reset their current relationships and inventory. They will arrive with their basic personality and voice settings, but they must start their social life on your island from scratch.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Heart} title="Relationship and Romance Management">

Managing the drama on your island is a full-time job. Relationships in the **rom tomodachi life vie de reve** edition follow a specific "status ladder." Knowing these levels helps you predict when a Mii is ready to confess their love or ask for a divorce.

| Relationship Tier | Status Label (NA) | Status Label (EU/FR) | Action Recommended |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Peak Romance** | Let's get married! | In Love | Use a Travel Ticket |
| **High Romance** | Super in love | Very Happy | Give a romantic gift |
| **Neutral Friend** | Getting along OK | All Right | Solve more problems |
| **Low Friend** | Not getting along | Not Great | Change their apartment |
| **Crisis** | (Fighting) | (Angry) | Mediate the fight immediately |

</Callout>
