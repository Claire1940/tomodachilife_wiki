## Daily Habits for Consistent Island Income

The foundation of a wealthy island is consistency. The game is designed to reward players who check in on their Miis daily. The most reliable source of passive income is the community fountain, located in the town square. Every day, your Miis will gather here to make donations to the island fund.

The amount you receive at the fountain depends on the number of Miis living in your apartment building and their overall happiness levels. A larger, happier population results in significantly higher daily yields.

| Activity | Frequency | Potential Earnings |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fountain Donations** | Daily | $20.00 - $100.00+ |
| **Apartment Visits** | Hourly | $1.00 - $10.00 |
| **Solving Mii Problems** | Unlimited | $1.00 - $5.00 per task |
| **Leveling Up Miis** | Per Level | $5.00 - $20.00 |

> **💡 Tip:** Always check the fountain first thing in the morning. If you miss a day, you miss out on that day's donation pool entirely, as they do not stack over time.

## Winning Mini-Games and Selling Prizes

Interacting with your Miis is one of the most active ways to build your bank account. When a Mii has a purple "game" icon in their window, they want to play a mini-game with you. These games include "Match," "Zoom Quiz," "Silhouette Quiz," and "Mii Facts." Winning these games allows you to choose between three different prize sizes: small, medium, and large.

While it is tempting to pick the large box every time, the items inside vary in their resale value. Most of these items are considered "Treasures" and serve no purpose other than being sold at the Pawn Shop.

### Common Mini-Game Rewards

| Prize Category | Example Items | Average Sale Price |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Small Prizes** | Tissue, Paper Crane, Ruined Meal | $1.00 - $5.00 |
| **Medium Prizes** | Kaleidoscope, Marbles, Yo-yo | $10.00 - $30.00 |
| **Large Prizes** | **Wolfy Cushion**, RC Car, Fine China | $40.00 - $80.00 |

To maximize your efficiency when learning **how to get money in Tomodachi Life**, focus on the Miis who specifically want to play games. If you are short on time, prioritize the "Zoom Quiz" or "Silhouette Quiz," as these are generally faster to complete than the "Match" card game.

## The Dream Strategy: Harvesting Items While Miis Sleep

One of the most overlooked methods for generating wealth involves the Miis' sleep cycles. When a Mii is asleep, you may see a thought bubble indicating they are dreaming. Entering these dreams not only provides a surreal look into their subconscious but also grants you a physical item once the dream concludes.

To use this strategy effectively, you can wait until nighttime or, if you are desperate for funds, adjust your system clock (though be warned that clock manipulation can temporarily disable seasonal events). Once a Mii is dreaming, watch the sequence and "tap out" or wait for it to end. You will receive a treasure related to that dream.

## Leveling Up Miis for Financial Rewards

Every time you feed a Mii, give them a new outfit, or solve their problems, they gain experience. When their experience bar fills up, they level up. This is a critical moment for your island's economy. When a Mii levels up, you are given the option to give them a gift, a song, or a new interior.

However, if you have already maximized their utility, you can simply enjoy the financial bonus that comes with the interaction. Furthermore, if you give a Mii a **Travel Ticket**, they will go on a vacation and return with a treasure. These treasures are often high-value items that can be flipped at the Pawn Shop for a significant profit.

### Strategies for Rapid Leveling
1.  **Identify Favorite Foods:** Feeding a Mii their "Super All-Time Favorite" food provides a massive experience boost and a cash bonus.
2.  **Solve Relationship Issues:** Helping Miis make friends or resolve fights provides more experience than simple hunger requests.
3.  **Clothing Rotations:** Even cheap clothing items provide an experience boost when given for the first time.

## Managing Your Island's Economy

To maintain a high balance, you must balance your spending with your earning. It is easy to blow all your cash on a "Luxury" interior that costs $1,000.00, but doing so will leave you unable to buy food or medicine for other Miis.

Follow these steps to ensure financial stability:
*   **Bulk Buy Basics:** Keep a stock of cheap foods like "Bread" or "Bananas" to solve hunger issues without spending a fortune.
*   **Save Travel Tickets:** Only use Travel Tickets when a Mii is unhappy or when you specifically need a high-value treasure to sell.
*   **Check the Morning Market:** The Morning Market at the Park often offers items at a discount, which can be a great way to save money on expensive food items.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the most expensive item you can sell in Tomodachi Life?**

A: The Diamond Ring is one of the most valuable treasures in the game, selling for $500.00 at the Pawn Shop. These are typically earned through high-level Mii interactions or as very rare rewards from large prize boxes.

**Q: Does the fountain donation amount ever increase?**

A: Yes, the amount of money you get from the fountain increases as your island's population grows and as the average happiness level of your Miis rises. Adding more Miis to your apartments is the best way to boost this daily income.

**Q: Can I get money from StreetPass or SpotPass?**

A: While StreetPass and SpotPass primarily bring new explorers or special clothing items to your island, the explorers themselves can contribute to your economy. When an explorer stays at your campsite, they may interact with your Miis and leave behind unique items that can be sold.

**Q: What happens if I sell an item a Mii gave me?**

A: If a Mii gives you a treasure as a gift for helping them, there are no negative consequences for selling it at the Pawn Shop. In fact, selling these treasures is the primary method for **how to get money in Tomodachi Life** to fund other aspects of the game.
