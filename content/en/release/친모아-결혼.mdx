### Steps to a Successful Proposal

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Accept the Proposal Request">
    When a Mii approaches you with a "love problem" indicating they want to propose, choose to help them. Declining or delaying too long might cause the request to disappear.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Choose the Proposal Setting">
    You'll select one of four locations for the proposal:
    - **Fireworks show**
    - **Train station**
    - **Amusement park**
    - **Fancy restaurant**
    Each location has unique visual elements and potential interruptions that can affect your timing. You can also choose if the proposer should change clothes for the event.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master the Minigame Timing">
    During the proposal scene, a thought bubble will appear above the partner's head. Tap the heart button **only when the partner is thinking about the proposer**. You need to get the timing right four times to succeed. Thought bubbles can switch quickly, so focus on accuracy rather than speed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Handle Child Couples (if applicable)">
    If the proposing Miis are children, a successful proposal will result in them receiving an "Age-o-matic" as a gift. They cannot complete the wedding without it. If you refuse to use it, the wedding will be called off.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### The Baby Journey: From Newborn to Traveler

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Baby Request and Acceptance">
    A married spouse will express a desire to have a baby. You can choose to approve or decline the request. If approved, the baby will arrive within one to two in-game days.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Newborn Customization">
    At birth, the mother will ask a series of questions to determine the baby's initial profile settings:
    - **Gender**: Choose Boy, Girl, or Random.
    - **Appearance**: The default is a mix of both parents' features.
    - **Name**: Accept a suggestion, ask for another, or enter your own.
    - **Personality**: Set as "like Dad," "like Mom," or "random."
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Six-Day Growth Cycle">
    Babies grow through visible daily stages for six days:
    - **Day 1**: Newborn, carried by parents or in a bassinet/stroller.
    - **Day 2**: Begins crawling.
    - **Day 3**: Hair starts growing, can stand briefly.
    - **Day 4**: Toddler stage, starts practicing walking.
    - **Day 5**: Walks, runs, and speaks in a higher-pitched voice.
    - **Day 6**: Fully grown and ready to move out.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Age-Appropriate Babysitting">
    Your babysitting actions will change with the child's age:
    - **Day 1**: Rhythmic rocking with the 3DS system.
    - **Days 2-3**: Head pats, belly tickles, and peekaboo.
    - **Days 4-5**: Circular stylus motions to lift and spin the child in the backyard.
    Parents will only call for help with crying babies during the first three days, and not after 8:00 PM.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Choosing the Child's Future">
    On day six, the parents will call you to decide the child's path:
    - **Resident**: The child moves into their own apartment on the island.
    - **Traveler**: The child becomes a traveler, waiting at the port for StreetPass boats to visit other islands.
    You get one final chance to edit the child's profile before they leave home.
  </Step>
</Steps>
