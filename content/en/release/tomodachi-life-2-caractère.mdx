## How to Influence Your Mii's Caractère
When creating or editing a Mii, the game asks you to rank five specific attributes. Your choices here determine the final **tomodachi life 2 caractère** result. 

1.  **Movement:** How fast does the Mii walk or move? (Slow to Fast)
2.  **Utterance:** How does the Mii speak? (Polite to Direct/Blunt)
3.  **Facial Expression:** How expressive is their face? (Flat to Varied)
4.  **Mindset:** Is the Mii logical or intuitive? (Serious to Relaxed)
5.  **Overall:** A general summary of their vibe. (Quirky to Normal)

> **Warning:** Changing a Mii's personality after they have established relationships can cause significant social friction. Friends may become "unfamiliar" and romantic interests might cool off.
