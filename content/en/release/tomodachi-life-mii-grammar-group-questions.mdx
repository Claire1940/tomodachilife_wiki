<Section icon={Swords} title="Personality and Mii Responses">
A Mii's personality is the primary driver behind their responses to group questions. There are 16 distinct personality types, categorized into four main groups: Easygoing, Outgoing, Independent, and Confident. Each group has a unique way of expressing thoughts and feelings, which directly impacts how they answer the "Bei wem in der Gruppe?" type of questions.

The personality chart is built from **Movement**, **Speech**, **Expressiveness**, and **Attitude** sliders. The "Overall" row is cosmetic and does not change the final personality result. These traits affect social chemistry, dialogue, animations, and even romance reactions.
</Callout>

|:------------------|:-----------|:-----------------------|:--------------------------|
