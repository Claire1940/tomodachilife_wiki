## How Sliders Determine Personality

The magic of the **persönlichkeiten tomodachi life 2** system lies in five specific sliders. Each slider has eight levels. Depending on where you set these, the game calculates the final personality type.

1.  **Movement:** How fast the Mii walks and moves. (Slow to Fast)
2.  **Speech:** The speed and tone of their voice. (Polite to Direct)
3.  **Expression:** How much their face changes. (Flat to Varied)
4.  **Attitude:** Their general outlook on life. (Serious to Quirky)
5.  **Overall:** Their general vibe. (Slow-paced to Quick-witted)

By experimenting with these combinations, you can unlock all 16 types. For example, a "Leader" usually requires high Movement and high Speech, while a "Thinker" requires low Movement and a more Serious Attitude.
