## Expanded Mii Creation and Customization

The heart of the experience remains the Mii Editor, but the tools available have seen a significant upgrade. In previous entries, players were often limited by the standard Mii Maker presets. In the current build, customization has been pushed to the extreme, allowing for characters that are either hyper-realistic or delightfully absurd.

One of the most talked-about additions is the "no features" option. Players can now create Miis with no eyes, no nose, or no mouth, opening the door for creative character designs that weren't possible before. Additionally, the skin tone palette has been expanded to include unnatural colors like blue and green, similar to the system seen in *Miitopia*.

## Relationship Dynamics and the Family Tree

Relationships in the original game were often a chaotic roll of the dice. While that randomness remains part of the charm, the new system introduces a much-needed layer of logic through the **Family Tree** feature. This allows you to define exactly how Miis are related to one another, preventing the awkward "romantic" pairings between family members that plagued earlier versions.

The depth of the family tree is impressive. You can now specify roles such as older/younger siblings, grandparents, and even distant relatives. This data directly influences how Miis interact and what kind of gifts or nicknames they share.

| Relationship Role | Impact on Gameplay |
|---|---|
| **Partner** | Enables specific romantic interactions and wedding event outfits. |
| **Sibling (Older/Younger)** | Influences shared activities and "sibling rivalry" dialogue. |
| **Grandparent/Grandkid** | Unlocks unique multi-generational gift-giving scenes. |
| **Relative** | Ensures the game recognizes biological ties for social logic. |

## New Gameplay Mechanics and Island Activities

The island itself has undergone a structural change. The traditional Town Hall building has been replaced with a streamlined pause menu, allowing players to access island management tools instantly without navigating across the map. This includes the new **Island Builder** and **Island Encyclopedia**, which track your progress and discoveries.

The Fountain, a staple of the series, now features a ranking system. As you contribute to the fountain and grant wishes, its rank increases, unlocking more complex activities for your Miis.

### Fountain Wish Categories
When a Mii visits the fountain to make a wish, you can choose from a variety of "Grantable Wishes" that trigger unique mini-games or stat boosts:
*   **Physical Fitness:** Options like "Yoga," "Bulk Your Bod," and "Football."
*   **Hobbies:** Options like "Guitar," "Camera," and "Baseball."
*   **Entertainment:** "Breaking DVD" or "Portable Gaming."
