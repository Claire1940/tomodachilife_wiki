## FAQ

**Q: Can I transfer my save from the original 3DS Tomodachi Life to the Switch version?**

A: No. Because *Tomodachi Life: Living the Dream* features a completely new engine, updated Mii models, and different personality matrices, direct save transfers from the 3DS are not supported. You must start fresh in the Switch demo or full game.

**Q: How many Miis can I have in the demo?**

A: The "Welcome Version" limits you to 3 Mii characters. Once you perform the **tomodachi life demo transfer to full game**, you will be able to expand your island to house up to 100 Miis.

**Q: Do I need a Nintendo Switch Online subscription to transfer my save?**

A: No, the save transfer happens locally on your console. However, if you want to use Cloud Saves to move your progress between two different Switch consoles, a Nintendo Switch Online subscription is required.

**Q: What happens if I ignore the demo and just start the full game?**

A: You will simply start from the beginning of the story. You will not miss any core gameplay features, but you will miss out on the exclusive Hamster Costume reward that is only granted to those who complete the demo version first.
