## FAQ

**Q: Does Tomodachi Life demo carry over if I start over on a new profile?**

A: Your new profile creates a separate island save. Transfer is usually tied to that specific profile, so your original profile’s island and new profile island are treated independently.

A: If local save data is deleted, that transfer candidate is typically gone. Reinstalling the demo alone does not normally restore deleted local progress unless a compatible backup exists.

**Q: Does Tomodachi Life demo carry over with all Mii edits made after the demo endpoint?**

A: It may, as long as those edits are on the same active save file tied to the profile you’ll use for the full game. Keep your “main” profile clean and avoid risky resets.
