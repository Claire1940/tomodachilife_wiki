## Troubleshooting Common Reset Issues

Sometimes, a standard deletion doesn't seem to work, or the game "remembers" your settings. This is often due to Cloud Saves or secondary profiles.

1.  **Cloud Save Interference:** If you have Nintendo Switch Online, your console might automatically download your old save from the cloud. Turn off "Automatic Save-Data Download" for the demo specifically before deleting.
2.  **Multiple Profiles:** If you want to keep your current island but try a new one, simply create a new User Profile on your Switch. Each profile has its own independent save slot for the demo.
3.  **Cache Clearing:** In rare cases, the Mii data might persist in the system cache. Navigate to System Settings > System > Formatting Options > Clear Cache if problems persist.
