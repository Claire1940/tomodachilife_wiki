  <Step num="2" title="Adjust Pixel Art Settings">

  <Step num="3" title="Generate Your Grid Template">

<Section icon={Swords} title="Recreating Designs In-Game with the Grid">
With your pixel art grid template ready, the next step is to meticulously transfer it into *Tomodachi Life*. This process requires patience but is made significantly easier by the detailed guidance provided by the "Living the Grid" tool.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access Design Editor">
    In *Tomodachi Life*, navigate to the design editor for Mii characters, pets, or other customizable items. Ensure you have a clear workspace and access to the in-game color palette.

  <Step num="2" title="Follow the Grid Template">

    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize In-Game Tools">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Review and Refine">
