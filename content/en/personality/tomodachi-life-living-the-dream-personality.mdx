<Section icon={Map} title="How to Create Specific Personalities">

Creating a specific **Tomodachi Life Living the Dream personality** for your Mii involves carefully adjusting the Movement, Speech, Energy, and Thinking sliders during their creation or modification. The 'Overall' slider does not influence personality, so you can ignore it for this purpose. The following steps outline the general process for setting up a Mii's personality.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access Mii Creation/Editing">
    When creating a new Mii, you'll reach the personality selection screen. If you wish to change an existing Mii's personality, navigate to your Residents menu, select the Mii, and choose "Personality" at the bottom.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Adjust the Four Sliders">
    You will see four sliders: Movement, Speech, Energy, and Thinking. Each slider has a range from "Slow" to "Quick" (Movement), "Polite" to "Honest" (Speech), "Flat" to "Varied" (Energy), and "Serious" to "Chill" (Thinking).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Match Slider Configurations">
    Refer to a personality chart or calculator (like the one below) to find the exact slider positions for your desired personality. Each personality has a unique combination of these four settings.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm and Observe">
    Once you've set the sliders, confirm your choices. The game will then assign the corresponding personality type to your Mii. Observe how their new traits influence their behavior and interactions on the island.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Experimentation is Key">
While specific slider configurations guarantee certain personalities, slight variations might still yield the same result. Don't be afraid to experiment if you're looking for a specific outcome not explicitly listed.
</Callout>
