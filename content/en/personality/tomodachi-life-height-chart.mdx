<Section icon={Target} title="How to Adjust Your Mii Height">

If you find that your islanders are too uniform, you can adjust their height at any time. This is particularly useful if you want to trigger more dramatic height chart events during future sleepovers.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Town Hall">
    Go to the **Town Hall** on your island map and select the **Mii Settings** option to view your full roster.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select the Islander">
    Choose the specific Mii you wish to edit and select the **Edit** button to enter the customization interface.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Modify Physical Appearance">
    Navigate to the **Height and Weight** tab (usually represented by a body icon). Move the vertical slider to your desired position.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Save and Sync">
    Confirm the changes. The Mii will immediately appear in their new size in their apartment and during island activities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Styling Tip">
Remember that weight is a separate slider. A "Tall and Thin" Mii will look significantly different from a "Tall and Large" Mii during the height chart event.
</Callout>

Changing height can also fix certain visual issues. For example, if a Mii's hat is constantly clipping through the ceiling of a specific interior, lowering their height by 10-15% on the scale can often resolve the "glitch" without ruining the character's look.

</Section>
