<Section icon={Map} title="How to Change Mii Personalities">
While you set a Mii's personality during creation, you're not locked into that choice forever. *Tomodachi Life* offers the flexibility to change a Mii's personality at any point, allowing you to fine-tune your island's social dynamics or simply experiment with new traits. This process is straightforward and can dramatically alter a Mii's behavior and interactions.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Residents Menu">
    From the main island screen, navigate to the **Residents** menu. This is typically accessible via the Town Hall or directly through the touch screen interface.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select the Desired Mii">
    Scroll through your list of residents and tap on the Mii whose personality you wish to change. This will bring up their individual Mii options screen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Choose 'Personality'">
    At the bottom of the Mii's options screen, you will find a button labeled "**Personality**". Select this option to enter the personality adjustment interface.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Adjust the Sliders">
    You will be presented with the four personality sliders: Movement, Speech, Energy, and Thinking. Adjust these sliders to achieve the desired personality type. The game will show you the resulting personality as you make changes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Confirm Changes">
    Once you are satisfied with the new personality, confirm your selection. The Mii's behavior and interactions will immediately update to reflect their new disposition, impacting **Tomodachi Life all personalities** on your island.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="No Permanent Consequences">
Changing a Mii's personality does not have any negative long-term consequences on their relationships or status. It's a tool for experimentation and personalization.
</Callout>

</Section>
