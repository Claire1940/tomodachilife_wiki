## How Tomodachi Life Mii Compatibility Works

At its core, the compatibility system in Tomodachi Life is a hidden algorithm that calculates a percentage score between two Miis. This score is primarily derived from the birth dates entered during Mii creation. The game uses a form of digital astrology, comparing the zodiac signs and "inner luck" of two individuals to see if they are a match.

It is important to note that a high compatibility score does not guarantee a relationship, nor does a low score prevent one. Instead, the score acts as a "buff" or "debuff" to the likelihood of positive interactions. Miis with high compatibility are more likely to have successful conversations, give gifts that the other likes, and successfully ask each other out on dates.

### The Compatibility Tester Unlock
To see these scores officially, you must unlock the Compatibility Tester building. This is achieved once you have at least two male and two female Miis on your island and have completed a certain number of social interactions. Once unlocked, you can check both "Friendship" and "Romance" ratings between any two Miis.

| Compatibility Range | Relationship Outlook | Social Impact |

> **Warning:** Even a 100% compatibility rating can be undermined if one Mii has a "Very Unhappy" status or if a third Mii interferes in a confession.

## Personality Types and Social Synergy

When you create a Mii, you answer five questions regarding their movement, speaking style, facial expressions, and overall attitude. These answers place the Mii into one of 16 personality types, categorized under four main umbrellas: Easygoing, Outgoing, Independent, and Confident. These categories significantly influence **tomodachi life mii compatibility** by determining how Miis react to specific social situations.

### The Four Personality Groups
1.  **Easygoing:** These Miis are relaxed and move at their own pace. They tend to get along with most types but can sometimes be "too chill" for Confident types.
2.  **Outgoing:** Social butterflies who love to talk. They have high synergy with other Outgoing Miis but may overwhelm Independent types.
3.  **Independent:** They prefer their own company or quiet activities. They are the hardest to "matchmake" but form very deep bonds once a connection is made.
4.  **Confident:** Bold and assertive. They take the lead in relationships, which can lead to quick romances or explosive arguments.

| Personality Group | Best Match | Worst Match | Social Behavior |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Easygoing** | Confident | Outgoing | Prefers indoor activities and slow conversations. |

| **Independent** | Independent | Confident | Often found alone in their rooms or at the beach. |
| **Confident** | Outgoing | Easygoing | High success rate in "confessing" love to others. |

To keep your Miis interacting and ensure you can test their relationships properly, follow these preventative steps based on known demo limitations:

1.  **Avoid the Clothing Store:** Unlocking the clothing store is a primary trigger for Miis becoming stuck. 2.  **Limit Your Mii Count:** Adding a third Mii in the demo often triggers the clothing store prompt automatically. If you want to keep exploring social interactions, consider staying with just two Miis for a longer period.
3.  **Monitor Dreaming:** If a Mii starts dreaming, they will often head to the fountain. While dreams are a fun mechanic, in the demo, they can sometimes lead to a loop that prevents other social interactions.

## Strategies to Improve Mii Relationships

If you find that your favorite pair has low **tomodachi life mii compatibility**, do not despair. While you cannot change their birth dates (the primary driver of the score), you can influence their actual relationship level through direct intervention.

### Using Travel Tickets
Travel Tickets are the most powerful items for boosting relationships. Sending two Miis on a trip together—whether they are just friends or a married couple—will almost always result in a significant boost to their relationship status. This can bypass a low compatibility score by forcing positive shared experiences.

### Food and Gifts
Giving a Mii their "All-Time Favorite" food boosts their happiness significantly. A happy Mii is more likely to have "green" (friendly) or "pink" (romantic) thought bubbles. Additionally, giving two Miis the same hobby item (like a Wii U or a Skateboard) increases the chance they will spend time together in the park or at each other's houses.

### The Power of "Look-Alike" Miis
The "Look-Alike" Mii (usually the player's personal Mii) has a special place in the island's social hierarchy. While they are still subject to the **tomodachi life mii compatibility** algorithm, they often receive more social prompts than other Miis. Use this to your advantage by making your Look-Alike the "social hub" of the island, introducing Miis to one another to spark new friendships.

| Action | Impact on Relationship | Recommended Frequency |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Give Travel Ticket** | Massive Boost | Save for "Relationship in Danger" status. |
| **Give Same Hobby Item** | Moderate Boost | Once per Mii pair. |
| **Solve Mii Problems** | Minor Happiness Boost | Every time a bubble appears. |
| **Level Up Gifts** | Variable | Give items that match their personality type. |

## FAQ

**Q: Does the Mii's favorite color affect tomodachi life mii compatibility?**

A: No, favorite colors do not influence the compatibility percentage. However, giving a Mii clothes or interior decor in their favorite color increases their happiness meter more than other colors, which indirectly helps their social life.

**Q: Can I change a Mii's birthday to fix a low compatibility score?**

A: Yes, you can edit a Mii's birthday at the Town Hall at any time. The **tomodachi life mii compatibility** score will update immediately in the Compatibility Tester. However, keep in mind that changing birthdays frequently can feel like "cheating" the game's life-sim mechanics!

**Q: Why do my Miis with 90% compatibility keep fighting?**

A: High compatibility only increases the *chance* of success. If one Mii has a "Confident" personality and the other is "Independent," they may still have clashing social styles. Additionally, if you neglect a Mii's hunger or health, they are more likely to be irritable regardless of their compatibility with others.

**Q: Is there a way to see compatibility without the building?**

A: Not accurately. Before the building is unlocked, you have to rely on visual cues, such as how often Miis visit each other's apartments or if they are seen together at the fountain or beach. The Compatibility Tester is the only way to see the raw numerical data.
