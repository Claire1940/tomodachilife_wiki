It's not always sunshine and rainbows on the island. Relationships can falter due, to various events.

| Event | Impact on Relationship | Prevention/Recovery |
|:------|:-----------------------|:--------------------|
| **Arguments** | Can lower friendship/romance status quickly. | Resolve disputes quickly by intervening. |
