The Easygoing group is characterized by a "live and let live" attitude. These Miis are rarely involved in major island drama and tend to be the most supportive friends. If you are looking for a Mii that gets along with almost everyone, this is the category to aim for.

### 1. Softie (Sweetie)
Softies are the emotional heart of any island. They are deeply in tune with the feelings of those around them and are known for being incredibly sentimental. To achieve this, set your sliders toward the "relaxed" and "gentle" ends of the spectrum.

### 2. Carer (Buddy)
The Carer is the ultimate friend. They are trustworthy and considerate, often putting the needs of the group above their own. They work tirelessly to ensure that everyone on the island is getting along.

### 3. Optimist (Cheerleader)
Always seen with a smile, the Optimist is the island’s beacon of positivity. They don't just smile for themselves; they actively try to lift the spirits of other Miis who might be feeling down.

### 4. Dreamer (Daydreamer)
Idealistic and romantic, the Dreamer often has their head in the clouds. While they might seem a bit detached from reality, they are the source of great creative ideas and whimsical dialogue.

## How to Configure Your Mii

To achieve specific results within the **tomodachi life living the dream all personalities** matrix, you must adjust the five sliders during the Mii creation or editing process. While the game doesn't show numerical values, think of each slider on a scale of 1 to 8 (Left to Right).

1.  **Movement:** How fast the Mii walks and moves.
2.  **Speech:** The speed and tone of their voice.
3.  **Facial Expression:** How expressive their face is when talking.
4.  **Attitude (Mind):** Their outlook on life, from polite to direct.
5.  **Overall:** Their general vibe, from quirky to normal.

> **Warning:** Changing a Mii's personality after they have formed relationships can drastically alter their compatibility scores with their spouse or best friend!
