<Section icon={Target} title="How to Get the Tomodachi Life Bubbly Personality">

Creating a Mii with the **tomodachi life bubbly personality** requires precise adjustment of the five personality sliders during the Mii creation process. These sliders—Movement, Speech, Facial Expression, Attitude, and Overall—determine the final classification. For the Bubbly type, you generally want to lean toward the right side of the sliders, indicating a fast, direct, and expressive nature.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Set Movement to Fast">
    The first slider controls how fast the Mii moves and acts. For a Bubbly personality, move this slider toward the right (Fast). Bubbly Miis are rarely slow or lethargic.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Adjust Speech to Direct">
    The second slider determines how the Mii speaks. Set this to the right side (Direct). This ensures the Mii is outspoken and confident in their verbal interactions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Make Expressions Varied">
    The third slider dictates facial expressiveness. A Bubbly Mii should have "Varied" expressions (Right side), showing their emotions clearly through their face.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Set Attitude to Outgoing">
    The fourth slider is the Attitude slider. Move this to the right (Outgoing). This is a critical component for the Energetic group classification.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Finalize with Overall: Quirky">
    The final slider, "Overall," should be set toward the "Unique" or "Quirky" side (Right side). This provides the final push into the Bubbly/Merry Maker category.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Slider Precision">
Even a single notch difference on one slider can accidentally shift your Mii into the "Adventurer" or "Charmer" category. Double-check your settings before confirming the Mii's personality.
</Callout>
